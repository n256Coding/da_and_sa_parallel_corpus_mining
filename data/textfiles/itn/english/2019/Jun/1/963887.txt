Minister Ravi Karunanayake says that steps will be taken to make the cost of production of a unit of electricity 15 or 16 rupees within two years.
The Minister mentioned this while on n observation tour of Aaantee Leco Company in Bandaragama.
Minister Ravi Karunanayake exchanged views with the workers of the company.
A group of people including Parliamentarians were present on the occasion.
