Five children survived a horror crash in Lima, Peru after a car lost control during a u-turn and rammed into minors on the footpath.
Officials reported that four girls were hit by the driver outside their school.
One of the victims is a three-year-old boy who was with his mother.
Local media reported the driver had made the u-turn at an intersection in the suburb of Miraflores when she lost control and mounted the footpath.
The children were rushed to hospital for treatment but are expected to make a recovery.
The driver has been taken into police custody.
