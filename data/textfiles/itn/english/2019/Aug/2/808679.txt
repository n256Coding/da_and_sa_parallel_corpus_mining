The Planting of Kap tradition for the historic Esala Perahera Festival took place at the auspicious time today at the four Maha Devales The Kap planting tradition took place at the Natha, Vishnu, Pattini and Kataragama Devales.
This is the first tradition of the Esala Maha Perahera Festival.
The receipt of rain at the proper time as well as prosperity is expected through the tradition.
Another aim of participating in the Perahera is to secure blessings for security and welfare.
The Basnayake Nilames and the Chiefs of the Devales were among the group that joined in the occasion.
