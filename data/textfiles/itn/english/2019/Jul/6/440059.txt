Minister Thalatha Athukorala says that two new Draft Bills will be presented in Parliament to prevent delays in laws.
The Minister said this during the opening ceremony of the High Court in Matale.
The opening of new High Court in Matale took place under the patronage of Minister Thalatha Athukorala.
Chief Justice Jayantha Jayasuriya and a group of people were present on the occasion.
Minister Thalatha Athukorala said that when inquired from the Prisons there is congestion due to unnecessary blocks in certain places.
She said that some more changes would be effected to 2 or 3 acts in future.
