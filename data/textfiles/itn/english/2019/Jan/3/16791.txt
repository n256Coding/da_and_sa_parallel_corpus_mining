New Navy Commander Vice Admiral Piyal de Silva has called on President Maithripala Sirisena.
Vice Admiral Piyal de Silva who assumed duties as the New Navy Commander met President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s official residence.
The New Navy Chief met the President after arriving at the President’s official residence fulfilling the tradition of meeting the Head of State after assuming duties in the new position.
An exchange of mementoes took place traditionally.
