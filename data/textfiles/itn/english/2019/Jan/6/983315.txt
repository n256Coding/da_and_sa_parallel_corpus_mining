Minister Gamini Jayawickrema Perera says the background has been created for a resurgence of economy and society.
The Minister told this to the media after meeting the Maha Nayaka of Malwathu Chapter Most Venerable Thibbatuwawe Sri Sumangala Thera.
Thereafter he called on the Maha Nayaka of Asgiri Chapter Most Venerable Warakagoda Gnanarathana Thera to receive his blessings.
