Pradeep Amirthanayagam has been appointed as the Deputy Chief of Staff of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
VCR Amirthanayagam is a fellow of the Chartered Marketing Institute of the U.K.
He has been a News Reader and Commentator for many years and has a great deal of experience in journalism, advertising and PR industries.
Amirthanayagam also used to be a Director of the Anura Bandaranaike foundation.
