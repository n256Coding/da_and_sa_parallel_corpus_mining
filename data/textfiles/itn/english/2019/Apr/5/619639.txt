A tense situation is reported from the Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha owing to a clash between the Pradeshiya Sabha members.
The clash had occurred over a proposal to give a shop at the newly built shopping complex in Bandaragama, to a certain family of the area.
The uproar had surfaced as the Bandaragama Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman had not fulfilled a promise to give a shop to a family that was residing on a land acquired for the construction of the shopping complex.
