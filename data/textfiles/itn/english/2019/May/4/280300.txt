Dutch rider Jesper Asselman held on to the blue leader’s jersey despite crossing 24th in stage two at the Tour de Yorkshire in what ended in sprint finish.
It was Rick Zabel of Germany who crossed the finish line first to take the stage putting him in second in the general classification, just one second behind Asselman.Asselman’s team mate Boy van Poppel is in third overall, five seconds behind the leader with two stages left to go.
