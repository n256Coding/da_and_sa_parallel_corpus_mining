President Maithripala Sirisena expressed his condolences to the relatives of sergeant S S Wijekumara who was killed while on a UN peacekeeping mission in Mali.
President Maithripala Sirisena went to the residence of late sergeant, S S Wijekumara at Pol-pithi-gama and expressed his condolences to the family members and the relatives.
The remains of two military personnel who were killed while on a UN peacekeeping mission in Mali are scheduled to be brought into the country tomorrow.
