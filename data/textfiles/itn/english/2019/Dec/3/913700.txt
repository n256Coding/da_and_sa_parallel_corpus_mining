Former Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe visited the MahanayakeTheras of Malwatta and Asgiriya today.
The former Premierexpressed his gratitude for the assistance rendered by the Maha Sangha during his tenure as Prime Minister.
He called on Tibbatuwawe Sri Sumangala Thera at the MalwathuMahaViharaya.
Subsequently, he met the Chief Prelate of the AsgiriMahanikaya Most Venerable WarakagodaGnanaratana Thero.
However the media was not allowed to cover the meeting with the MahanayakaTheras.
