“The Kindness of Strangers” is a 2019 internationally co-produced drama film, written and directed by Lone Scherfig.
It stars Andrea Riseborough, Zoe Kazan, Tahar Rahim, Bill Nighy and Jay Baruchel.
Principal photography began in March 2018, and took place in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, New York City, and Copenhagen, Denmark.
The Kindness of Strangers will be released this Friday.
