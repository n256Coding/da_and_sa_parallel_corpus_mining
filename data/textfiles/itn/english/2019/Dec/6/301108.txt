The Australian High Commissioner in Sri Lanka told the President that Australia is committed to providing the highest level of support to Sri Lanka.
Australian High Commissioner to Sri Lanka David Holley met President Gotabhaya Rajapaksa at the Presidential Secretariat.
The High Commissioner conveyed his best wishes of the Australian Government to Gotabhaya Rajapaksa.
It was also discussed at this meeting to further strengthening of bilateral ties between the two countries.
