The ceremony organised by ITN Media Network in connection with the World Children’s Day took place at Gampaha Henarathgoda Botanical Gardens.
Several programmes organized on behalf of the children took place on the occasion.
ITN Media Network offered gifts to the children who won various events.
Deputy General Manager Programmes of ITN, Indrajith Wanniarachchi and group of people attended the ceremony.
Another programme was held at the same place to recognize the teachers in connection with the Teachers’ Day which falls today.
Special gifts were given to the teachers at the ceremony.
