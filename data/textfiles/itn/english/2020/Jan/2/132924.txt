Rehan Adithiya of Badulla Central College was able to raise the pride of the motherland to the world.
He won the gold medal in the International Mathematical Olympiad Competition in Tokyo, Japan, surpassing 162 countries.
Before the Olympiad final in Japan, competitions were held in Hong Kong and Thailand with the participation of 162 countries.
Rehan was able to emerge victorious by defeating eleven competitors including eight Gold medalists.
The young Sri Lankan genius was able to score 144 out of 180 in this tournament.
Rehan Aditya, returned home yesterday with his exceptional wins.
Rehan Adithya Weerakoon is a Grade Seven student of Badulla Central College.
