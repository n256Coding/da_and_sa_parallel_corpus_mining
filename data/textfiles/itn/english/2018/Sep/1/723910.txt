Erecting of a fence to a distance 4,750 meters to prevent accidents by falling into Viyaana Canal, has been completed.
Several lives were lost during the past after vehicles fell into Viyaana Canal in Maiyanganaya.
President Maithripala Sirisena ordered to erect a fence immediately to prevent the accidents.
