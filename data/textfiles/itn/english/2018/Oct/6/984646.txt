The Summit under the theme Indian Ocean Defines our Future, will be held on the 11th and 12th of this month at the Temple Trees in Colombo.
President Maithripala Sirisena is scheduled to make the inaugural address at the Summit on the 11th of this month.
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe will deliver the Keynote Address of the Summit.
The UN Special Representative on Oceans Peter Thompson is scheduled to address the summit.
It has been jointly organized by the Prime Minister’s office, Foreign Affairs Ministry, Lakshman Kadirgamar Institute and the UN’s office on Narcotics and Crimes.
