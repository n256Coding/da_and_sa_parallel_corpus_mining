The budget of the Bope Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha has been passed with a majority of three votes.
A group of United National Party members had arrived at the Pradeshiya Sabha attired in black.
Expressing their protest they attempted to set the budget report on fire.
As a result a tense situation surfaced.
However, the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna succeeded in getting the budget of the Bope Poddala Pradeshiya Sabha approved.
