Water bowsers have been distributed to be used during the drought season.
The bowsers were distributed under the patronage of Minster Rauf Hakeem.
This Project has been launched by the Ministry of Water Supply and Drainage Board.
23 water bowsers are due to be distributed spending a sum of 234 million rupees.
Of this, ten bowsers were handed over at the Ministry today.
These bowsers are due to be used in several Districts including Trincomalee, Batticaloa, Kurunegala and Moneragala
