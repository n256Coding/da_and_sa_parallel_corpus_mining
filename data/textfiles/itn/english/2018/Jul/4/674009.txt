President Maithripala Sirisena paid his last respects to late senior journalist, Chamara Lakshan Kumara.President Sirisena visited Chamara Lakshan’s home in Matugama and paid his last respects.
Chamara Lakshan was the Editor in Chief of ‘Silumina’ and ‘Resa’ newspapers.
The President extended his condolences to wife and son of Chamara Lakshan.
The final rites took place this afternoon.
