The case filed against former Minister Basil Rajapaksa and his relative Thiru Nadesan alleging that they have misused state funds to the tune of 202 million rupees and building a house after purchasing a land in the Malwana area was taken up again for hearing today.
The case was heard in the Gampaha High Court.
The second witness Muditha Jayakody gave evidence today.
He said that Basil Rajapaksa instructed him to examine a land in the Malwana area and inform him of the suitability of building a house there.
The Court permitted Basil Rajapaksa to travel abroad for medical treatment.
Further hearing was postponed for the 03rd of next month.
