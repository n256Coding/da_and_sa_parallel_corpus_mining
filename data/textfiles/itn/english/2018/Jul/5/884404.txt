CCTV footage with respect to the shooting targeting the Chief Incumbent of the Kirivehera Raja Maha Vihare the Ven.
Kobawaka Dhamminda Thera has been received.
The Chief Sanghanayake of the Ruhuna Magampattuwa and Chief Incumbent of the Kirivehera Raja Maha Vihare the Ven.
Kobawaka Dhamminda Thera and another monk of the Vihare were injured in the shootout.
Both monks are receiving treatment and are recovering.
