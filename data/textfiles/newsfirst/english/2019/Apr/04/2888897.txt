COLOMBO (News 1st) – Five suspects including actor Ryan Van Rooyen, who were arrested with notorious underworld drug kingpin “Makandure Madush” in Dubai, have been deported.
Police spokesperson SP Ruwan Gunasekara noted the group had been taken into the custody of the Criminal Investigation Department upon arrival at the Bandaranaike International Airport and are being interrogated.
Thirteen individuals in total who were arrested in the Dubai operation have been deported to Sri Lanka until now.
Three of them were detained and released.
Additionally, organized crime gang leader “Kanjipani Imran” is being detained and questioned by the Colombo Crimes Division.
Police stated that three others were produced before courts and subsequently remanded, while another was produced before the court and released on bail.
