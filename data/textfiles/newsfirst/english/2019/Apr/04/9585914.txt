COLOMBO (News 1st) –  Another Gammedda project commenced today(April 04) at Puwakpitiya.
The foundation stone was laid for sanitary facilities at the Tamil Primary school in Puwakpitiya, Homagama.
The funds for the project were provided by T.J. Lanka Group of companies.
Six hundred and twelve (621) students are currently attending the Tamil Primary school.
The event was graced by the senior management of The Capital Maharaja Organisation.
