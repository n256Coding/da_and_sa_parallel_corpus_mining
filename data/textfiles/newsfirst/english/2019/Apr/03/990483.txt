COLOMBO (News 1st) – A drug dealer who was was selling Heroin via “eZ cash” was arrested in Horana.
The police stated that the suspect had rented three houses in Horana and Welikala in order to run the drug racket.
Police found 25 grams of Heroin in his possession.
The 33-year-old suspect, a resident of Rathnapura was arrested while he was traveling in a car.
It was revealed that the police had filed cases against him in connection to jewelry heists and drug trafficking in the past.
