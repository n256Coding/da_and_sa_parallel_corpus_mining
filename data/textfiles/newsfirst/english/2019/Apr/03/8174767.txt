Colombo (News 1st): Police arrested a 33-year-old for impersonating an Astrologer and an Ayurvedic Doctor using fake identification.
Police said the suspect had operated an Ayurvedic Centre in Balangoda from the 1st of January and had treated patients for various illnesses.
Police seized a passport, fake identification card, a variety of ointments and other material from the suspect’s possession.
