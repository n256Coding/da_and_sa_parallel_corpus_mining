Colombo (News 1st) -A rally organised by the Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna was held in Hatton yesterday.
Bimal Rathnayake stated that most of the ministers own estates and some names of ministers were mentioned.
They were Thondaman, Suresh, Rishard Bathiudeen, Zoysa, Seneviratne and mentioned that half of the cabinet ministers own estates.
He further stated that Thondaman was named as king-maker and that he has got the vote of the people to make kings.
He noted that they have not done anything to develop the estate but instead they have made themselves kings of estates.
