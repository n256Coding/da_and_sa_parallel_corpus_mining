COLOMBO (News 1st) – Bus fares along the Southern Expressway have been slashed by Rs 20 from today.
Issuing a communique, the National Transport Commission said bus services will be made available from the new Multi-Modal Transport Center at Makumbura, which was declared open yesterday by President Maithripala Sirisena.
The bus fares have been revised in line with the launch of the services.
