Colombo (News1st): An individual was murdered in Pottuvil following an argument between two brothers.
The elder brother had used a bottle to assault his younger brother.
The younger sibling was severely injured and succumbed to his injuries following admission to the Hospital.
The deceased is a 24-year-old and his remains are currently placed at the morgue of the Pottuvil Base Hospital.
The murder had been committed after an argument relating to a land dispute escalated.
