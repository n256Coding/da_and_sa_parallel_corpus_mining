Colombo (News1st) – President Ibrahim Mohamed Solih is currently on a three-day unofficial visit to Sri Lanka.
According to News1st sources, the president is in Sri Lanka with his family for a short holiday.
President’s Spokesperson Ibrahim Hoodh revealed that President Solih will return to the Maldives on 06th January.
President Solih is yet to make an official visit to Sri Lanka after assuming duties as the President.
He arrived in Sri Lankan shortly before being sworn in, to accompany former President Mohammad Nasheed back to the Maldives.
