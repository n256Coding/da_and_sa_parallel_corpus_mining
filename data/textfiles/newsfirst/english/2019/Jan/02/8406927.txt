COLOMBO (News 1st) – Colombo Chief Magistrate Lanka Jayarathne noted that a decision on a case against MP Johnston Fernando will be given on the 26th of January.
The case was filed by the bribery commission for failure to disclose his assets and liabilities in 2010 and 2011.
Five cases of similar nature have been filed by the bribery commission against MP Johnston Fernando from 2010 – 2014.
