COLOMBO (News 1st):- The Civil Aviation Authority states that daily commercial flights between Jaffna International airport and the Chennai and Trichy airports in India will commence from the 10th of this month.
Director-General, H. M. M. C. Nimalsiri says that the flights will be operated by an Indian Airline.
In addition, a locally owned airline has also scheduled flights three times a week from Jaffna to airports located in South India.
