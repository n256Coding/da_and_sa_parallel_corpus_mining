COLOMBO (News 1st):- Three Sri Lankans died in a motor accident in Madagascar.
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated that the unfortunate incident occurred last night (November 1).
The three victims died as a result of their vehicle falling into a river.
The bodies were discovered and retrieved by the Madagascar police.
The bodies currently lie at a hospital in Madagascar.
The Ministry stated that the High Commissioner for Sri Lanka in Seychelles is probing into more information about the incident.
