COLOMBO (News 1st):- The Nugegoda fly-over has been temporarily shut down for maintenance activities.
Thereby, the police urge drivers to make use of alternative roads when travelling.
The area surrounding the Nugegoda fly-over was heavily congested this morning due to the closure of this fly-over.
The Chairman of the Road Development Authority, Nihal Sooriyarachchi said that they are looking to end the maintenance activities that commenced last night (November 1) as soon as possible.
