Colombo (News 1st): The Joint Postal Trade Union Alliance states that all services have been hampered for five days due to a computer breakdown.
Its Convener Chinthaka Bandara said that as a result of this the revenue of the department has also fallen.
When News 1st inquired from Minister of Postal Services and Muslim Religious Affairs Abdul Haleem regarding the matter, he noted that steps are being taken to restore the computer system back to normalcy.
