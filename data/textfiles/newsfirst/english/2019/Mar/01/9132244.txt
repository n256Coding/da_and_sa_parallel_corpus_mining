Colombo (News 1st): State Minister Ranjan Ramanayaka joined the list of MPs who released their asset declarations to the public domain today (March 1).
He released this declaration of assets and liabilities through Transparency International Sri Lanka (TISL).
Yesterday five members of parliament from across party lines came together to publish their declarations of assets and liabilities in the public domain.
The group included Tharaka Balasuriya, Vasudeva Nanayakkara, M.A.
Sumanthiran, Vidura Wickramanayake and State Minister Eran Wickramaratne.
