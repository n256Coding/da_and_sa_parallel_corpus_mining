COLOMBO (News 1st) – The promotional tour of the Abhiman-Waram Scholarship Program is planned to cover the Eastern and Central Provinces today (July 04).
The ‘Abhinam Waram’ is to recognize and appreciate talented youth and it is powered by Anchor and Champion Network.
A group is touring the Central Province and they will conduct the awareness program in Nuwara Eliya today.
They are scheduled to arrive at Thawalamathenna, Kotmale and in Kandy also today.
Another group in the Eastern province is raising awareness in Ampara and the programme commenced from the Sadatissa Maha Vidyalaya.
