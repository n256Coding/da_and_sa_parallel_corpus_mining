Colombo (News 1st): Three suspects aged 21, 31 and 42 were arrested for the possession of a firearm manufactured overseas at George Perera Mawatha, Grandpass.
Police noted that they were arrested following a raid carried out by the officials of the Organized Crimes Division.
The arrested are residents of Gokarella, Ibbagamuwa and Colombo.
The suspects are due to be produced before the court today (July 01).
