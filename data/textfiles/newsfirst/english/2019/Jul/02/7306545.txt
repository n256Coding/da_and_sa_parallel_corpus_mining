Colombo (News 1st): Two men were arrested by Police for the possession of a total of 32 grams of heroin in Ratmalana and Avissawella.
A 28-year-old man from Ratmalana was arrested with 12.53 grams of heroin and will be produced at the Mount Lavinia Magistrate’s Court.
A 44-year-old was arrested at Avissawella for the possession of 20.2 grams of heroin and he will be produced at the Maligakanda Magistrate’s Court.
