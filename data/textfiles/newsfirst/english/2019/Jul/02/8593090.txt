COLOMBO (News 1st) – Two suspects were arrested with over 32g of heroin in Avissawella and Ratmalana.
Police stated that one of the suspects was arrested in Mt.
Lavinia with 12.53g of heroin.
The suspect is a 28-year-old resident of Moratuwa and he is to be produced before the Mt.
Lavinia Magistrate court today(July 02).
The other suspect was arrested at a bus stop in Avissawella with 20.2g of heroin.
The suspect is a 44-year-old resident of Wellampitiya and he is to be produced before the Maligakanda Magistrate court today(July 02).
