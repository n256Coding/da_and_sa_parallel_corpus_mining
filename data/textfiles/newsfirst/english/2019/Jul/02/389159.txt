COLOMBO (News 1st) –  The foundation stone for the construction of a new two-story building at the army head office in Narahenpita was laid yesterday under the auspices of the Army Commander, Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayaka.
The building upon completion will provide accommodation to the officers attached to the Army Headquarters.
Following a colourful procession, the Army Commander, Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayaka planted a sapling.
