Colombo(News1st) – General Secretary of UPFA MP Mahinda Amaraweera stated that disciplinary inquiries into the members of the SLFP who joined other parties will be carried out expeditiously and will be reported to the Election Commission.
He further stated that the members of the Parliament, as well as those of Pradeshiya Sabha, are included in the aforesaid procedures.
MP Mahinda Amaraweera added that the President will make a special statement on future party plans at the party’s anniversary celebrations tomorrow (3 September).
