Colombo (News1st): The Kurunegala High Court delivered a verdict today (Feb 01) sentencing a school teacher to two years of rigorous imprisonment on charges of assaulting a student in 2011.
The suspect is a 55-year-old teacher at Nagarasanna College, Kurunegala.
High Court Judge Menaka Wijesundera ordered the convicted teacher to pay Rs.
100,000 as compensation to the student.
