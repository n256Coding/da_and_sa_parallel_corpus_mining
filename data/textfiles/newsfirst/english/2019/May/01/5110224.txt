Colombo (News 1st): The report submitted by the special three-member committee appointed to probe the Easter Sunday terror attacks, will be made available to all parties according to President Maithripala Sirisena.
The President said that he will table the report in parliament and implement the defence plan for the future.
The President said that he is due to receive the report of the committee that was appointed with the highest judicial powers, within two weeks.
President had made these statements at a gathering where senior defence officials met after the event where new defence secretary General Shantha Kotteggoda received his letter of appointment at the Defence Ministry.
