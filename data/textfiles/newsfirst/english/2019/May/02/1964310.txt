Colombo (News 1st): The police conducted a raid on the house of a close associate of Mohamed Kissam Mohomad Rilwan who died in the blast in Saindamarthu, Kalmunai.
Police stated that the uncle and aunt of the suspect resided in this house in Ariyampati, Batticaloa.
The two suspects, a suicide vest, mobile phones and ATM cards were taken into police custody.
