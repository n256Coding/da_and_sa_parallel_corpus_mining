Colombo (News 1st): Eleven people have been arrested during a search operation conducted in Wattegama, Kandy.
Police noted that they seized a bore 12 firearm without a licence, 10 knives, a camera and video recordings when they searched a house.
The search operations were conducted by the Sri Lanka Army and the Police.
Meanwhile, another search operation was conducted in the Nittambuwa Thihariya police division.
