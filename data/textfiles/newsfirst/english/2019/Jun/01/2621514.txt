COLOMBO (News 1st): A woman died while another was admitted to the general hospital in Matara after being attacked with a chemical.
A person has been arrested on suspicion of entering the house and attacking the women with the chemical.
The police said that investigations are also underway to arrest a woman who is also a suspect.
The murder was due to a land dispute between the two parties.
The chemical used for the crime has been identified as a chemical used to produce jewellery.
Further investigations are ongoing.
