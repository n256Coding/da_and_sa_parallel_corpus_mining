Colombo (News 1st): The All Ceylon Graduate Teachers’ Association said it will step away from Advanced Level Examination paper marking duties if the recommendations of the B.C.
Perera salaries commission report is not implemented.
In a letter to the Minister of Education, the All Ceylon Graduate Teachers’ Association said they expect the Ministry to announce its stance to them on or before the 15th of August.
News 1st’s attempts to contact officials from the Ministry of Education for a comment on this matter proved futile.
