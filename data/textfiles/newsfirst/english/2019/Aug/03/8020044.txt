COLOMBO (News 1st): 6956 individuals have been arrested in islandwide DUI raids conducted within a period of 29 days.
Police media spokesperson SP Ruwan Gunasekara said that the islandwide raids commenced on the 5th of last month.
166 individuals were arrested for driving under the influence within the last 24 hours that ended at 6.00 am this morning (August 3).
