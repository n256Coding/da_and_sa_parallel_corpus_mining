COLOMBO (News 1st): A 45-year-old person has been shot dead a short while ago (August 3) in the Kuleegoda junction of Ambalangoda.
The person who sustained injuries in the shooting died when he was being admitted to the Balapitiya Hospital.
The deceased has been identified as the chief jailer of the Welikada Prison training school.
The police has commenced investigations into the incident.
