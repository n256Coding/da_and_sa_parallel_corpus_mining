Colombo (News 1st): Sirasa TV and Sirasa Movies won many awards and accolades at the 2019 Signis Tele Cinema Awards.
Nadeeshani Henderson, best known for her role as Udumbara in the movie Udumbara, won the Best Excellence Award.
Thaaththa, which aired on Sirasa TV, received a number of awards.
The awards ceremony was held at the BMICH yesterday (August 2).
Ricardo Younes and His Eminence Malilo Vegara Bishop of the Philippines joined as the chief guests.
