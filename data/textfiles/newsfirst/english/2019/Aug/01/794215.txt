Colombo (News1st) –  Three suspects had been arrested in Koswatta, Bandaragama on the charges of abducting and assaulting an individual.
Police stated that the victim had been abducted by the victim’s employer regarding a monetary fraud.
The driver of the vehicle used in the abduction had been arrested and the vehicle was seized by the police.
The suspects aged 38, 42, 51 were reported to be residents of Bandaragama.
Further investigations are underway to apprehend the main suspect of the incident who had fled the location, according to the police.
