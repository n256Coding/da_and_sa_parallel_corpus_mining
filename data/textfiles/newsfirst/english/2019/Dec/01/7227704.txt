COLOMBO (News 1st) – State Minister of Home Affairs and Provincial Councils, Janaka Bandara Tennakoon stated that the ministers who have not yet handed over official quarters and official vehicles should hand over the state-owned property to the Ministry of Public Administration and Home Affairs as soon as possible.
He added, he will be looking into the progress of this process tomorrow (December 02) and steps will be taken to deliver official residences and official vehicles to new Ministers who were appointed.
