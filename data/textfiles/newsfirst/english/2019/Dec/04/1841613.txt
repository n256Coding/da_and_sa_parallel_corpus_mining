Colombo (News 1st): The Ambassador of the United Arab Emirates in Sri Lanka Ahmad Ali Al Mualla and a delegation headed by the New Zealand High Commissioner to Sri Lanka Joanna Kempkers called on President Gotabaya Rajapaksa yesterday (December 3).
The Ambassador of the UAE assured President Rajapaksa his country’s commitment to further enhance bilateral relations between the two countries.
Meanwhile, the delegation headed by the New Zealand High Commissioner to Sri Lanka assured their country’s commitment to enhance mutual benefits by further developing bilateral relations.
