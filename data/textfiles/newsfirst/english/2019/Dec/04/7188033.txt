COLOMBO (News 1st) – Allirajah Subaskaran, the Sri Lankan born Chairman of Lycamobile and Lyca Group of Companies, has been conferred an honorary doctorate by the Amist University of Malaysia.
The special doctorate has been awarded to recognize the establishment and operation of the flagship Lyca Company in more than 20 countries in the international telecommunications industry and for the humanitarian work that is being funded by Lyca’s Gnanam Foundation.
Lycamobile, which was launched in 2006 by Allirajah Subaskaran now operates in more than 20 countries including the United Kingdom and the United States of America.
