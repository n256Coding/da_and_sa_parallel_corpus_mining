16 Bank Accounts in two leading Private Banks in Sri Lanka linked to Former Sri Lankan Ambassador to Russia Udayanga Weeratunga have been frozen based on an order issued by Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne.
The Accounts are said to contain a total of 1.5 Million US Dollars.
Investigations were conducted by the Police FCID in to the 16 bank accounts linked to Udayanga Weeratunga.
The order was issued when the Police FCID informed court about the bank details through a motion.
The motion was presented during the court case in to the alleged misappropriating funds by Udayanga Weeratunga through a deal to purchase MiG Fighter Aircraft for the Sri Lanka Air Force during the former administration.
