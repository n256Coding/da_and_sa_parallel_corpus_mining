Tuesday, July 4 – President Maithripala Sirisena has handed over appointment letters to three top-positions in the state.
The appointment letters were given for the positions of Secretary to the President, Defence Secretary and Army Commander.
Those appointed to the above positions are as follows; Austin Fernando is a former Governor to the Eastern Province and a Senior Administrative Officer.
Kapila Waidyarathne served as a Senior Additional Solicitor General prior to his appointment as the Defence Secretary.
Also, Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake, the new Army Commander served as the Military Chief of Staff.
