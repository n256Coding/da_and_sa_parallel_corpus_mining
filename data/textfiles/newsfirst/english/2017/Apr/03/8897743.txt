The National Water Supply and Drainage Board says that the water supply to several areas in Colombo will be suspended for a period of 18 hours starting from 9 a.m. tomorrow, April 04.
Accordingly, water supply will be suspended in Kosgas junction, Grandpass, Mahawatte, Thotalanga in Baseline road from the Kelani bridge to Dematagoda junction and all by-roads connecting to it.
The water cut is the  result of repair work at the Ambatale water plant.
