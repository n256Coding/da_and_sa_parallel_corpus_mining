Ravi Jayawardene, the son of  late Sri Lankan President, J. R. Jayawardene, has passed away aged 80.
Ravi Jayawardene who was the founder of the Police Special Task Force, was also a specialist in defence.
Late Ravi Jayawardene worked as the Security Advisor to President J. R. Jayawardene, during the UNP regime of 1980 and he was also employed as a pilot for SriLanka Airlines.
He also represented Sri Lanka at the Olympics in shooting events.
