COLOMBO (News 1st) – The treasurer of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party Minister S. B.Dissanayake says the parliamentary members of SLFP and UPFA will support the No-confidence motion against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
According to Minister S.B.
Dissanayake, all members of the parliament who are representing SLFP and UPFA have decided to vote for the No-confidence motion.
He further said a party meeting is scheduled to be held on 2 nd April to come to the final decision.
According to him irrespective of results of the no-confidence motion a new government will be formed with relevant amendments to the cabinet on 04 th April.
