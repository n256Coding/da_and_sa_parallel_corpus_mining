COLOMBO (News 1st) – Notorious underworld figure Angoda Lokka’s main lieutenant has been arrested.
Police Special Task Force (STF) said the suspect, Sarath Kumara, was arrested at his residence in Kotikawatta this morning (April 3).
Police STF personnel also seized a grenade in the possession of the 41-year-old suspect.
He is scheduled to be handed over to the Organised Crimes Division, today (April 3).
