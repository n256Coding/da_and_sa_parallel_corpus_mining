COLOMBO (News 1st) – TNA Parliamentarian Charles Nirmalanathan says TNA MPs should not attend tomorrow’s (April 4) debate on the Motion of No Confidence against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
He said regardless of who committed the offense, be it the President or Prime Minister, if it is proved, they have to step down from their positions.
Further, he added that this is the true definition of Good Governance.
He went onto note he will raise this proposal at the party’s meeting today (April 3) and hopes all 15 members of Parliament of the TNA will a reach a decision on this.
