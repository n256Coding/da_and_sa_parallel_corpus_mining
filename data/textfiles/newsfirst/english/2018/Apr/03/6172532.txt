COLOMBO (News 1st) – Today (April 3) marks day 4 of the protest march from Kandy to Colombo, undertaken in support of the no-confidence motion against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
The 4th day of the march commenced from Nittambuwa.
The march has been organised by the organisation to protect the UNP together with the United National Freedom Front.
The march is being held under the theme, “The Power Struggle to Defeat Dictatorial Cronyism” .
