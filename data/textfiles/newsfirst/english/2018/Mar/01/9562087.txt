COLOMBO (News 1 st ): Three suspects have been arrested today (March 01) in Dematagoda while in possession of  Heroin.
Police had discovered 500 grams of Heroin which, according to Police Anti Narcotic Bureau, is worth over Rs.
6 Million.
The arrested suspects are residents of Moratuwa and Mattakkuliya, Police said.
Police added that the suspects will be produced before the Maligakanda Magistrates Court.
