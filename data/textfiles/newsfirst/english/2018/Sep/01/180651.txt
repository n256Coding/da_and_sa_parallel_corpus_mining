Colombo (News1st) – One suspect was arrested in Kadawatha on Friday (Aug 31st) with a stock of heroin worth over Rs.
8.8mn.
A total of 740.145 grams of heroin was discovered in the suspect’s possession.
The police anti-narcotics bureau made the arrest last night as the suspect was transporting the drugs on a motorbike.
The Police also took into custody a total of Rs.
233,920 that was in the suspect’s possession.
