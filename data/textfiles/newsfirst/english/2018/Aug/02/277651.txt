COLOMBO (News 1st) – A suspect was arrested on the Oluthoduwai coast in Mannar for possession of 130 kilograms of Kerala Ganja.
A spokesperson for the police stated that the Police STF had carried out the raid according to a tip-off received by the SL Navy.
The Kerala Ganja worth Rs.
6.5mn had been found hidden in four sacks.
The suspect has been identified as a 39-year-old resident from Mannar.
The suspect is due to be presented before the Mannar magistrate’s court.
