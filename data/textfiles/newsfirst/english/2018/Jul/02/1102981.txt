COLOMBO (News 1st) – 29 new houses were handed over to estate workers in Arappala Kanda Dodangoda.
The event was attended by Minister Rajitha Senarathne.
Addressing the gathering Minister of Health, Rajitha Senarathne stated that those people who enjoy the nutrition subsidies granted to them do not know where it comes from.
The Minister also continued to say that the country people had been led to believe that everything was done by the Rajapakse’s because that is what the media reports.
“But the Rajapakshe’s took more than what they gave.
So they didn’t have money to give” urged Senarathne.
