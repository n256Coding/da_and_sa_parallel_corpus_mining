Reuters: More than 190 anxious family members from Indonesia of those perished in the crashed Indonesian plane are waiting at the information center for the updates of the accident.
The relatives and friends of victims are here to collect the personal belongings of their beloved ones who unfortunately took the ill-fated Lion Air Boeing 737 Max 8, which crashed on Monday during its flight from Jakarta to Tanjung Pinang in Bangka Belitung province, with 181 passengers and 8 crew members onboard.
The police officers have required them to provide all personal information and objects of the passengers in order to identify the bodies found by search teams, including their height and blood type.
