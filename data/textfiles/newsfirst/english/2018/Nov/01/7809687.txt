Colombo (News 1st) – Thibbatuwawe Sri Siddhartha Sumangala Thero of the Malwathu chapter talking to the media stated that three and a half years have gone by for the President and that during this time period MP’s are switching sides without realizing what people are going through.
The Mahanayake thero stated that the government needs a proper framework and that according to his opinion, only MP Sajith Premadasa is working according to a proper agenda and nobody else works towards the betterment of the people.
