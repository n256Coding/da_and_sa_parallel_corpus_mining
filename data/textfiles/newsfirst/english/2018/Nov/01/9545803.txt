Colombo (News 1st) – A group of UPFA MPs met with President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat today.
UPFA MP  Vasudeva Nanayakkara talking to journalists stated that they discussed matters pertaining to the activities in Parliament.
When asked whether the Parliament will be convened on 05th of November, MP Vasudeva stated that it is not confirmed yet.
He also stated that Prime Minister MR did not make any such statement and that he only said “maybe” it will be convened.
MP Udaya Gammanpila when questioned on Parliament being convened on the 05th said the decision has not been made yet and that it is the decision of the President.
