Colombo (News1st): Bishop of Kurunegala His eminence most Rev.
(Dr.) Harold Anthony Perera said the people are confused at this moment due to the various arguments and opinions that are being put forward regarding the present political and administrative crisis.
He strongly believes the Politicians and State Officers must come together to solve this matter within the country itself.
He added as Sri Lanka does not interfere with the internal matters of other nations, other nations should also not interfere with the internal affairs of Sri Lanka.
