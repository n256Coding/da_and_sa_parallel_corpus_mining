COLOMBO (News1st) – Dinesh Gunawardene has been appointed as the Minister of Megapolis and Western Development.
Vasudeva Nanayakkara was sworn in as the Minister of National Integration, Reconciliation, and Official Languages.
Keheliya Rambukwella was appointed as the State Minister of Mass Media and Digital Infrastructure while UNP MP R.D.
Ashoka Priyantha was sworn in as Deputy Minister of Cultural and Internal Affairs, and Regional Development (Wayamba).
This was confirmed through Media Spokesperson for the government Keheliya Rambukwella.
