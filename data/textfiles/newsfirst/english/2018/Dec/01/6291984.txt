Colombo (News 1st) – Three-wheel prices will be reduced by Rs.
10 with effect from Midnight tomorrow (December 02).
Sunil Jayawardena, Chairman of the National Association of Self-employed Professionals said that the charge for the first Kilometre was reduced from Rs.
60 to Rs.
50.
He noted that this change came about due to the reduction of fuel prices.
He further said that the Consumer Affairs Authority will take legal action against drivers who do not reduce the three wheel prices by midnight tomorrow.
