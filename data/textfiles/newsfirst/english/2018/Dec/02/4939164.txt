COLOMBO (News 1st) – A group of Sri Lankans employed at a private company named JNP in Saudi Arabia suffers immense difficulties due to the non-payment of salaries throughout the past six months.
The workers said that the government should intervene in getting them the due payments and in bringing them back to the island.
When News First inquired from the Sri Lankan embassy in Saudi Arabia, an official said that they have informed Saudi’s Ministry of Labour of the matter and that immediate steps will be taken to resolve it.
