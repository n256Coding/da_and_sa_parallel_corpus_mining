COLOMBO (News 1st) – The Prime Minister’s office has announced that a special statement is to be made by Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapakse tonight (December 02).
In his statement, he will be addressing the current political issues of the country.
The Prime Minister will also explain the powers vested in the President to dissolve parliament.
