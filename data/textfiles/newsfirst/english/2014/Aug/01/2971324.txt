Police say that they are yet to receive any information regarding the abduction of a four-year-old boy in the Katugampola area in Meegalewa, Galgamuwa.
The Police Media Unit stated that five teams have been deployed to investigate the incident.
The unit also noted that statements have been recorded from several individuals in this regard.
The child was abducted by four individuals who arrived on two motorbikes.
The parents of the boy were injured in the incident.
