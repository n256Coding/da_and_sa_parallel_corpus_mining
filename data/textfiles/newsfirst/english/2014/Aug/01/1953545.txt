The annual festival of the iconic Nallur Kovil in Jaffna commenced on August 1.
The commencement of the festival was marked with the flag raising ceremony.
Nallur Kovil in Jaffna holds special prominence for Hindu devotees.
The flag raising ceremony commenced following religious observances held at dawn.
Special poojas and other religious rituals will be performed daily until August 25 as part of the annual festival of the Nallur Kovil.
The Ther festival or the Chariot Festival will be held at the auspicious time on August 24.
