The body of a 55-year-old witness in the case where a politician allegedly made a teacher of a school in Nawagaththegama in Puttlam kneel before her class, has been found.
Police Media Spokesperson SSP Ajith Rohana said the body was found in an abandoned well, noting that the body was recovered after the magistrate was called to the scene.
The police media spokesperson said there are several witnesses in the case, noting however, the deceased was the chief witness.
