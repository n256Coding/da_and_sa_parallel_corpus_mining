A group of armed thieves who broke into the home of a businessman in Nilpanagoda, Minuwangoda, has made off with cash and jewellery worth about Rs.
5 million.
The police say that three of the thieves, armed with a pistol and swords, had broken into the house at about 7:45 p.m. on July 31.
The gang of thieves, comprising seven persons in total, had threatened to kill the inhabitants of the house and had made off with the cash and jewellery.
No leads have come to light as yet regarding the suspects.
