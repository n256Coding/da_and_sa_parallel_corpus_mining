Today marks the first year anniversary of the Democratic Party.
This milestone was celebrated under the auspices of Sarath Fonseka – the leader of the party.
The celebration was organised at the Democratic Party Office in Kotte.
A group of individuals including the Deputy Leader of the party Jayantha Ketagoda and the Treasurer, Anoma Fonseka attended the event.
Since its inception on April 1,  last year the Democratic Party has participated in two provincial council elections.
The first was the North Western and Central Provincial Council Polls and the second was the recently concluded Western and Southern Provincial Council Polls.
The number of seats that the Democratic Party had secured in both provincial council elections stands at 17.
