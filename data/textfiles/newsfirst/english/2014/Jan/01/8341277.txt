Police have launched extensive investigations into the incident where an infant’s body was found near the Hirana Bridge in Panadura.
The police said that the body was found Wednesday morning according to a tip-off.
The police further added, that the infant’s body which had been a few days old, had been mauled by animals.
According to a high-ranking police officer, it is suspected that the infant’s body has been dumped there after it had been brought from another area.
