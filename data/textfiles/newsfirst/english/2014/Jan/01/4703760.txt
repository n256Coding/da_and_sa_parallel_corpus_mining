Extending his wishes for the year 2014, President Mahinda Rajapaksa says that the new year will be of special significance in economic and national development as well as in the field of politics in Sri Lanka.
The president points out that we should continue to move towards this while ensuring national unity, peace and satisfaction.
He added that this has been identified as the correct path for true prosperity of the nation and building of the country.
Meanwhile, in his new year message,Prime Minister D. M. Jayaratne says that the new year is an opportunity to achieve property and eternal unity in the motherland.
He added that we who made many development goals a reality must focus on achieving spiritual goals this year.
