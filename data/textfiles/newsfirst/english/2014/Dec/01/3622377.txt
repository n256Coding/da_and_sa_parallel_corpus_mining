Common Opposition Candidate at the upcoming presidential election Maithripala Sirisena is scheduled to enter into an agreement with parties who will express their support for him at the impending polls.
The signing will take place at the Viharamahadevi open air theatre.
MP Dr. Rajitha Senaratne stated that the function will be attended by parties and organisations pledging their support for Maithrjpala Sirisena.
Dr. Senaratne also stated that another agreement is to be signed with the Jathika Hela Urumaya later Monday.
