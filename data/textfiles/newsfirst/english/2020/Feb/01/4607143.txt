COLOMBO (News 1st):- The Sri Lanka Navy has discovered a haul of over 97 kg of Kerala Ganja during a patrol conducted in the sea area close to Mayiladi, Jaffna.
The haul of Kerala Ganja was discovered upon inspecting a couple of suspicious parcels that were floating in the sea.
The haul of KG has been handed over to the Police STF in Jaffna for onward investigations.
