The SLTB and private bus fares will be increased from today, August 1.
The National Transport Commission published the amended bus fares on July 22.
Thereby the fares of ordinary, semi-luxury and luxury buses will be increased from today.The minimum bus fare has been increased from eight to nine rupees, while the maximum fare has been increased to Rs.690 from Rs.651.
Deputy Minister of Transport Ashok Abeysinghe stated that the bus fares have been increased by six percent.
