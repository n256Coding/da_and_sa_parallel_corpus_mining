Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe is scheduled to depart for Indonesia on Monday on a two-day official visit.
The visit comes in order to attend the 12th World Islamic Economic Forum.
The prime minister’s office stated that the Prime Minister is scheduled to meet with Malaysian President Joko Widodo,  the Indonesian Maritime Affairs Minister and others,  to conduct bilateral discussions.
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was also invited to deliver a keynote address in the opening session of the 12th World Islamic Economic Forum where the Prime Minister of Malaysia, the President of Pakistan and Deputy Minister of Jordan and other world leaders are scheduled to meet.
The Prime Minister’s office also stated that a number of ministers are also scheduled to make this tour with the prime minister.
