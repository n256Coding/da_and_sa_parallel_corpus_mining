The train schedules of the Sagarika express train travelling from Matara to Maradana have been revised.
Thereby, the train service starting at 4.55 a.m.  from Matara is scheduled to reach the Galle Railway Station at 5.44.
According to Sri Lanka Railways the Sagarika express train service will depart from the Galle station at 5.55 a.m. and reach the Maradana Railway station at 8.16 a.m.
