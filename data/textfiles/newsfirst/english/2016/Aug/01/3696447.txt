Former Minister of Economic Development Basil Rajapaksa, has been further remanded until the 8th of August.
This order was issued when Basil Rajapaksa was produced before Kaduwela Magistrate Dhammika Hemapala on Monday morning.
The Former Minister was brought to the Kaduwela Magistrate Court in an Ambulance today.
Following an inspection carried out by the Magistrate, orders were issued to further remand the Former Minister.
Basil Rajapaksa was arrested by the Police FCID and remanded on the 18th of July for the alleged misappropriation of funds belonging to the Devi Neguma Department.
