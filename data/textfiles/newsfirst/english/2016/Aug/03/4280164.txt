German midfielder, Leroy Sane has completed his move to Manchester City for £37m plus add-ons and has signed a five-year contract after leaving Schalke.
“He is a special talent and an exciting player that I think our supporters will enjoy watching”, said City manager Pep Guardiola The 20-year-old helped Germany reach the semi-finals at Euro 2016, scored eight goals in 33 Bundesliga appearances last season as Schalke finished fifth.
