“There would have easily been a gathering of over 20 million people at least”, is what UPFA MP Geetha Kumarasinghe said at a media briefing held in Ambalangoda on Thursday August 4.
The parliamentarian says that at the beginning,  the walk which had millions of people, walked from Colombo to Kandy.
Whereas the march actually happened the other way around.
Following that statement, she went on to comment on the number of individuals, which according to Geetha Kumarasinghe, is “ at least over 20 million people” .
According to the Department of Census and Statistics, as of 2012 the population count of Sri Lanka is 20, 277, 597.
