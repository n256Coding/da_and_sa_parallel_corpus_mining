The annual Facets Gem and Jewellery Show 2016 commenced under the auspices of President Maithripala Sirisena at the BMICH on Thursday, September 1.
The exhibition, which is organised by the National Gem and Jewellery Authority is being held for the 26th time.
Over 200 local and foreign exhibitors will be showcasing their products at the exhibition, which will continue until September 4.
Upon inaugurating the exhibition, President Maithripala Sirisena inspected the exhibits and engaged in cordial discussions with the staff.
