President of the Mahabodi Society of Sri Lanka, Ven.
Bangala Upatissa Thero attended a workshop on Commerce and Trade that is being held on the sidelines of the Indian Ocean Conference 2016 in Singapore.
The workshop was chaired by former Indian Ambassador to Sri Lanka Ashok K. Kantha.
It also saw the participation of the Chairman of the Institute of Policy Studies in Sri Lanka Dr. Razeen Sally.
