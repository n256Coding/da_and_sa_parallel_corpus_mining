The police headquarters have warned that there could be a potentially high degree of traffic until Monday, 4th of January owing to the public returning to Colombo and other densely populated areas.
According to Police Media spokesperson ASP Ruwan Gunasekara, the IGP and all senior officials, including Deputy Inspector General have been advised to take appropriate measures, in order to reduce the heavy influx of traffic.
He added that the IGP had issued special orders to maintain public peace during this phase.
