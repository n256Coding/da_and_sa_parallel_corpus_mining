The Sri Lanka Computer Emergency Readiness Team, says that it received 2800 Facebook related complaints over the last year.
Senior Information Security Engineer, Roshan Chandragupta stated that most of the the complaints received were related to fake FB accounts.
He went on to note that the public should be cautious when adding unknown persons to ones Facebook friend list.
