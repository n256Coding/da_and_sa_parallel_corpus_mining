Invoking blessings to the nation in view of the New Year,  the Chief Prelate of the Siam Sect of the Asgiriya Chapter the most venerable Galagama Sri Aththadassi Thero made a special statement.
Most Venerable Galagama Sri Aththadassi Thero, making a special statement said that as the new year has dawned, we need to keep away from harmful acts and works towards bestowing peace and prosperity on the nation.
