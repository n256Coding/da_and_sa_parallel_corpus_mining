At least six persons have been injured following a high speed collision between two buses at Panchikawatta junction in Maradana on Friday morning.
News1st’s Krishan Devasagayam reported on the incident.
When News 1st contacted the police, they said that the number of injured has risen to 13, and that the injuries are minor.
Police added that the wreckage has been moved from the site, and that the traffic flow in the area has returned to normalcy.
