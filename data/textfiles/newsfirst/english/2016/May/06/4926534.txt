Court refused bail to an individual who is said to have made death threats against the president and the prime minister via social media platforms.
The suspect was produced before Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris on Friday.
The Criminal Investigation Division said their investigation will be interrupted if the suspect was granted bail.
The magistrate also ordered for the suspects mobile phone and other accessories to be directed to the University of Colombo School of Computing so that a report may be obtained.
The magistrate ordered that the suspect be remanded until May 20.
