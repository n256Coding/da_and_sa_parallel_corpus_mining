Mawbima , a daily national newspaper, won several international awards at Goafest 2016 , held recently in Goa India.
The Mawbima Newspaper won three gold awards at the event.
The award was for a advertisement published in the Mawbima newspaper aimed at the Vesak festivities by Sarva Integrated.
General Manager of Ceylon Newspapers (Pvt) Ltd, Manel Dharmakeerthi said that in the newspaper industry today, Mawbima rising with more creative ideas, and these accolades, show that the Mawbima Newspaper is moving ahead in popularity, sales and creativity in this industry today.
