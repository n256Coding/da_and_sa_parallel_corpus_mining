The Asia Shipping Conference 2016 , titled Emerging Trends in Shipping and Logistics – Asia connects organized by the Women’s International Shipping and Trading Association or WISTA and the Ceylon Association of Shipping agents or CASA, was held in Colombo on Friday May 6.
News 1st was the official electronic media sponsor for the event.
The event was held under the auspices of Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr. Harsha de Silva.
Making a special note during the conference, Minister Dr. Harsha de Silva said that Sri Lanka has become one of the most protectionist economies in the world.
The minister said: We have been marketing ourselves as one of the most open economies in the world.
