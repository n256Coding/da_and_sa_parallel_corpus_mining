Shots were fired at a prison department bus in Dematagoda which was transporting suspected underworld leader  Dematagoda Chaminda.
Chaminda is a suspect connected to the killing of Baratha Lakshman Premachandra.
Commissioner General of Prisons Nishan Danasingha confirmed that the prisons department bus had come under attack .
Speaking to News 1st Prisons Spokesperson Thushara Upuldeniya said that Dematgoda Chaminda was injured in the shooting and was rushed to hospital.
Prisons Spokesperson went on to say that shots were fired at the bus.
