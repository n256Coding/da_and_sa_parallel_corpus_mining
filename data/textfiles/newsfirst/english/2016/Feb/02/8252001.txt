Two lorries, which were transporting teak exceeding the amount as provisioned in the licence, were taken into custody by Galgamuwa forest range officials.
Our correspondent stated that the stock of teak which were being transported from Anuradhapura to Moratuwa were around three hundred thousand rupees more than the licensed amount.
The Galgamuwa Police have arrested the four suspects and seized the two lorries they were travelling in,.
The suspects will be produced before the Galgamuwa Magistrate’s Court today.
