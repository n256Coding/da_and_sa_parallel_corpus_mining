Speaking at an event held in Kandy on Monday, former President Mahinda Rajapaksa stated that he will not leave the Sri Lanka Freedom Party.
The former president made this statement upon his arrival at the Ogastawatte Wijeyarama Temple in Kandy.
The former president said that they were under the impression that massive changes have come to pass but, this change has taken a different nature, adding that revenge and vengeance is beginning to spread.
