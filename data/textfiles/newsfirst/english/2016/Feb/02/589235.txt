The former Assistant Superintendent of Police of Embilipitiya, C. Dharmaratne has been arrested by the Criminal Investigations Department over the death of a youth during a party in Embilipitiya.
The Criminal Investigations Department said that Former ASP of Embilipitiya C. Dharmaratne was arrested after he was summoned to the CID on Tuesday afternoon.
The former Assistant Superintendent of Police is scheduled to be produced before the Embilipitiya Magistrate’s Court on Wednesday.
