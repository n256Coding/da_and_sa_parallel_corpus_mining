The public opinion poll for the constitutional reforms commenced in the Kandy and Gampaha Districts on Monday, February 1.
Chairman of the Public Representations Committee on Constitutional Reforms, Lal Wijenayake said that the poll will be conducted on Monday and Tuesday at the Kandy and Gampaha district secretariats.
He stated that votes can be cast in the opinion poll via phone calls, fax, e-mail and postal services.
