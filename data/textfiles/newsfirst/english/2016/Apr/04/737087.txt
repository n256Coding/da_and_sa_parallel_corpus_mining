The Department of Meteorology comes bearing good news for all Sri Lankans as they announce that the Inter-monsoon conditions are establishing over the country.
The conditions are expected to reduce the dry humid weather which has made many suffer during the past few months.
According to the Met Department showers or thundershowers would occur at several places except Eastern Province after 2.00 p.m.
