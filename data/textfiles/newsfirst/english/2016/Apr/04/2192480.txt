Samantha Kumara alias Wele Suda who is currently serving a death sentence after being convicted for his involvement in a drug racket,  was produced before the Colombo Chief Magistrate’s Court on Monday, April 4.
Seven suspects including Samantha Kumara alias Wela Suda were produced before Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya for the hearing of a case filed under the Money Laundering Act.
At the hearing, the Chief Magistrate ordered that the reports on the investigations carried out into the incident be submitted to courts on June 10.
The Magistrate ordered for the suspects to be further remanded until such time.
