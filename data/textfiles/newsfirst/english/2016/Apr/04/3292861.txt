Convening a media briefing on Monday, MP Vasudeva Nanayakkara responded to the statement made by the Prime Minister, stating that while the Sri Lanka Navy themselves does not have the required naval power to protect Sri Lanka’s waters, the Sri Lanka Navy is interested in taking up the responsibility of safeguarding sea routes in international waters The General Secretary for the Democratic Left Front stated that the Prime Minister says that the necessary equipment, ships and helicopters to serve this purpose will be provided to the Sri Lanka Navy.
“Now this is a rather suspicious factor”, he charged who pointed out that this is seen as a statement as the beginning of a programme to obtain military aid for the autocratic interests of the United States.
