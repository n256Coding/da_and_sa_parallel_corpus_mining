With the Sinhala Tamil New Year festive season in the horizon, the Sri Lanka Transport Board (SLTB) launched a special programme for the safety of commuters.
According to SLTB Deputy General Manager,  Raja Gunathilake, CCTV cameras will be functioning around the central bus stand.
DGM Gunathilake also noted that two buses with GPS facilities will be introduced during the season.
Furthermore, a hotline has been introduced for  people to lodge complaints,  if required.Complaints could be lodged through 0117 -555- 555
