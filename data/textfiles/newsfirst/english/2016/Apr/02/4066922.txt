Police Headquarters has taken steps to establish a new division under the name Central Criminal Intelligence Analysis Bureau.
Police Media Spokesperson ASP Ruwan Gunasekera said that the unit will be established in the office of the Criminal Records Division.
Data pertaining to severe crime being reported on a daily basis will be compiled using modern technology.
The new police division will be entrusted with the task of analysing this data, and informing investigators on the latest developments of criminal activity.
