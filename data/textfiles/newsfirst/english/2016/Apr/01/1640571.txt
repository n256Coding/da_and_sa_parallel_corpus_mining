Today is day three of the “Yovun Pura” youth camp themed Future Begins .
The camp is organised by the National Youth Services Council The Yovun Pura Camp has been revived after a lapse of 27 years.
It will be an oasis which will promote discipline, personality, leadership and educational qualities of youth of both sexes.
A number of performaces were staged by the youth on Friday morning.
The camp will conclude on April 3.
