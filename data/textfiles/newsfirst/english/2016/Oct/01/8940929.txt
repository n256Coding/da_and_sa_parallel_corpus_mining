The Department of Examinations has said that the results of the Grade Five Scholarship Examination will be released before October 15, 2016.
The Commissioner General of Examinations W.M.N.J.
Pushpakumara said that they have completed the marking of the exam answer scripts.
“The results are currently being uploaded onto the website”, he said.
He went on to note that, 350,701 students sat for the Grade Five Scholarship Examination this year in 2,959 examination centres countrywide.
