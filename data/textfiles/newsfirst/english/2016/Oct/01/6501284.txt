Four persons have been arrested in Mukkam, Kochchikade for  allegedly sand mining without a valid licence.
Police said equipment used in the operation were also taken into custody and that suspects aged 31, 38, 21 and 37 are from Kochchikade, Katana and Waikkal.
They were uncovered in a raid conducted by officers at the STF Headquarters in Mukkam, upon receiving a tip-off.
On Friday, September 30, two children died after falling into a water pit in Kochchikade, Negombo as a result of illegal sand-mining.
