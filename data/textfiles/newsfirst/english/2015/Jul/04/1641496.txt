President Maithripala Sirsena says no one would be allowed to drive back the silent revolution and the aspiration of the people that took place on the 8th of January.
The President made this statement at the commencement of the construction on the section of the southern express way from Godagama to Hambantota.
At the event the President emphasized that he operates and takes decisions in a manner that would secure the transformation that took place on the 8th of January.
