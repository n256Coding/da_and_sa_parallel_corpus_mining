Deputy Minister of Policy Planning and Economic Affairs Harsha de Silva said he believes President Maithripala Sirisena would not turn back on the mandate of 6.2 million people in the country.
The deputy minister made this statement at an awareness rally held in Obeysekerapura, Rajagiriya.
He said that there is confusion in politics today, adding that a conspiracy was afoot at the residence of Susil Premajayantha.
He went on to note that some are trying to intimidate President Maithripala Sirisena and make former president Mahinda Rajapaksa the candidate.
