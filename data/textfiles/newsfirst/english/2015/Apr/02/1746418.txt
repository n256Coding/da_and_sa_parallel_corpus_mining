The results of the enrollment examination to employ persons to the fishing industry in South Korea will be released on Thursday, April 2.
The Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment said that the results could be obtained via their website www.slbfe.lk.
2,104 candidates out of 2,220 applicants sat for the examination.
Over 50% of those who pass the examination, which was held on March 28, will be deployed to South Korea for employment.
The Foreign Employment Bureau said that applicants who possess experience and other professional qualifications in the fishing industry will be considered as a special qualification during the interview following the examination.
