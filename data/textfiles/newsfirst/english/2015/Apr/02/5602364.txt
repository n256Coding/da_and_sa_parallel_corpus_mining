Minister of Finance Ravi Karunanayake says that despite the government’s economic plan being heavily criticized by some, the people have expressed a positive response to it.
The Finance Minister speaking on the 100-day programme said: It’s 67 days today since the rule of the new government.
And within these 67 days of the 100-day programme we have managed complete 78% of the projects.
The minister made this statement while attending an inauguration ceremony of a programme to provide one million jobs.
A website which will help find information on jobs, was also launched by the minister.
