Thirty seven (37) Indian fishermen who were arrested by Sri Lankan authorities off the seas of Kankesanthurai have been remanded until April 17.
The group of Indian fishermen who were arrested by the Sri Lanka Navy on Friday evening were produced before the Point Pedro Magistrate on Saturday.
Court ordered that the fishermen be remanded until April 17.
Five fishing vessels which were used by the fishermen were also taken into custody by the Navy.
Meanwhile six Sri Lankan fishermen were arrested by Indian authorities 154 nautical miles off the coast of Chennai.
