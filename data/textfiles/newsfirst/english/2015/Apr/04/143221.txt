A vehicle dived into the Dambarawa Oya near the Dambarawa bridge killing one individual and injuring another.
According to the police, the vehicle was travelling towards Mahiyangana from Badulla when it veered off the road when the driver lost control, and fell into the Dambarawa Oya Police said that four passengers who were seriously injured were admitted to the hospital.
One passenger succumbed to his injuries.
The deceased person was a 53 year old resident of Udubaddawa in Dummalasuriya.
