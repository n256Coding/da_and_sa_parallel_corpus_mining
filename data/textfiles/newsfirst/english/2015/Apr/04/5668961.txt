The Navy states that two Japanese warships arrived at the Colombo Port on Saturday April 4.
Navy Media Spokesperson, Commander Indika de Silva said that the warships signify the strengthening bi-lateral relationships between the two countries.
He  also stated that a number of Japanese navy officers and sailors are aboard the vessels.
The two warships are scheduled to weigh anchor from the Colombo Port on the evening of  Sunday, April 5.
