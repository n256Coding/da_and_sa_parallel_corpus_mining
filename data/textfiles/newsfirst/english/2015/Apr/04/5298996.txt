The Water Supply and Drainage Board says that a water cut will come into effect in the Gampaha District from 8 a.m. to 5  p.m. on Sunday.
Thereby, the nine hour water cut will be implemented in areas that fall under the Kelaniya, Wattala, Hendela.
Biyagama, Mahara Pradeshiya Sabhas and also in areas that belong to the Dompe Pradeshiya Sabha such as Deketana, Namaluwe, Kalukodhayawa and Dompe.
In addition the water cut will also come into effect in Peliyagoda, Wattala and Ja-ela areas that fall under the purview of the Katunayake – Seeduwa Urban Council.
The Water Supply and Drainage Board further notes that the a water cut will be implemented from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Sunday in the Biyagama Investment Zone as well.
The water cut will be implemented on Sunday, as a result of a disruption of power supply to the Biyagama refinery.
