Former Air force Commander, Air Chief Marshal Donald Perera is being interrogated by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division.
Police Media Spokesperson ASP Ruwan Gunasekara stated that Perera is being questioned on alleged financial irregularities in the purchase of 04 MIG aircraft for the Air Force, in 2006 and the financial loss incurred in the repairing of several other aircraft belonging to the SLAF.
ASP Gunasekara noted that the transaction resulted in losses amounting to more than Rs.14 million.
