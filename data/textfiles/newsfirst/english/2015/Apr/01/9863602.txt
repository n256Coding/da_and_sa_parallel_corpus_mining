Relatives of Sri Lankan migrant workers in Yemen have urged the Sri Lankan Government to set up a proper procedure to repatriate their loved ones.
International media reported today that Houti rebels have intensified their attack on the Capital of Sanna which continues for a 7th consecutive day on Wednesday (April 1).
The relatives and families of the migrant workers who met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Managala Samaraweera had requested him to assure the safety of the Sri Lankan workers.
International media reports that both India and China have taken steps to repatriate their migrant workers.
