Police investigations have been launched into the suspicious death of an Indian couple inside a hotel in Wellawatte.
Police Media Spokeperson ASP Ruwan Gunesekera said that the cause of the death will be traced, following the magisterial inquest into the bodies which is scheduled to be conducted today, Friday, April 3.
He also added that an investigation will also get underway to find out whether the couple had consumed poison prior to their death.
Police say that the bodies of the couple were recovered following a tip-off received on Thursday night.
The couple had arrived in the country on March 27.
