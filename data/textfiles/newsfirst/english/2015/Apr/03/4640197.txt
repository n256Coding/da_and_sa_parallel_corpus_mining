Announcing the cabinet decision to the media, Cabinet Spokesperson Minister Rajitha Senaratne said that the 19th Amendment to the Constitution will come in to effect with the changes in the electoral system.
The cabinet spokesperson stated that the SLFP has reached a decision to include an article which says that this amendment will come into effect with the change in the electoral system.
He added that no-one will be allowed to simply jump-over the electoral system.
