President Maithripala Sirisena visited the historic Mahiyangana Raja Maha Viharaya on Sunday evening and participated in religious observances.
Upon arriving at the Mahiyangana Raja Maha Viharaya, the President called on the Chief Incumbent, Venerable Urulewaththe Dhammarakkitha Thera and received his blessings.
President Maithripala Sirisena then participated in religious observances.
