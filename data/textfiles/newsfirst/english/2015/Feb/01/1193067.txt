The Nawam Perehera of the Hunupitiya Gangarama Viharaya in Colombo will take to the streets on Monday and Tuesday.
Venerable Kirinde Assajji thero stated that all preparations have been made for the Perahera.
The annual Perahera celebrations will be held for the 37th time this year.
News1st was able to witness the preparations being made in and around the iconic temple.
The Perahera will take to the streets at 7 p.m. tomorrow and the day after.
