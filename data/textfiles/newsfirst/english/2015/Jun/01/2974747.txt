Speaking at a public meeting held in Matara, the Deputy Minister of Civil Aviation, Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena stated that the passage of the 20th Amendment to the Constitution is essential if democracy is to be safeguarded in the country.
The Deputy Minister noted that there must be a change in the ‘corrupt’ electoral system.
He added that both the district system and the proportional representation system must be brought into the country as it is important to strengthen the country’s democracy for future generations.
