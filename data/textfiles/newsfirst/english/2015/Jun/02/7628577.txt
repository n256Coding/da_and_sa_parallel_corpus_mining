Forty seven people were hospitalised after being attacked by wasps at a temple in the Uma-ela Area in Welimada, Uva Paranagama earlier Tuesday.
According to Police Media Spokesperson, ASP Ruwan Gunasekera, the wasps nest had been disturbed by strong winds.
The injured devotees who were present at the time of the incident occurred have been admitted to the Uva Paranagama Base Hospital.
