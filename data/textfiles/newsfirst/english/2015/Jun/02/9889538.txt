Meanwhile, 20 people were injured in a motor accident involving a private bus in the Beragala area in Haputale.
Police said the accident occurred when the driver of the bus, which was plying from Colombo to Badulla, had fallen asleep on the wheel and ran into an earth embankment on the left side of the road at around 9:20 p.m.
The injured have been admitted to hospitals in Haputale.
