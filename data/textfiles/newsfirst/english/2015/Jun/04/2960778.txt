Investigations have been launched to locate an individual who attempted to abducted a ten-year-old girl in the Gorakaduwa, Pelawatta area in Meegahathenna.
The police noted that the child was rescued by the residents of the area during the attempt.
The suspect had subsequently fled the scene.
Police added that a special team has been assigned to apprehend the suspect, who has been identified as a forty-three-year old resident of the same area.
