Three Sri Lankans are among the top scorers of the 2015 ICC Cricket World Cup.
At the very top sits Sri Lankan centurion Kumar Sangakkara who has scored 268 runs in 4 matches with a scoring average of 134.00.
The power hitter from West Indies, Chris Gayle is on 2nd with 258 runs in 4 matches, just 10 runs away from Sangakkara.
Sri Lankan opening pair takes third and fourth places as Lahiru Thirimanne who has 256 runs to his name sits at number three and Tillakaratne Dilshan takes 4th with 229 runs.
Shikhar Dhawan of India stands in 5th place with 224 runs.
