The Presidential Commission to investigate into complaints regarding missing persons is set to conduct its next hearing at the district Secretariat office in Trincomalee today, Monday March 2.
The Secretary of the Commission, H.W.
Gunadasa confirmed that the hearing would take place on Monday and Tuesday.
The Presidential Commission to investigate  into complaints regarding missing persons conducted hearing on Saturday and Sunday at the Kuchchaveli Divisional Secretariat.
The  commission has thus far received a total of twenty thousand one hundred and ten complaints.
