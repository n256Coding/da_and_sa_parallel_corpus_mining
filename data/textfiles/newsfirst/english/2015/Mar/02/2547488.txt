United Nations Under Secretary-General for Political Affairs Jeffrey Feltman is due to tour Jaffna today, Monday March 3.
According to the Ministry of External Affairs,  Jeffrey Feltman is due to hold discussions with the governor and chief minister of the Northern province.
Feltman is also due to meet with Prime MInister Ranil Wickramesinghe today.
The United Nations Under Secretary-General for Political Affairs arrived in the country on a four-day official visit on Saturday.
