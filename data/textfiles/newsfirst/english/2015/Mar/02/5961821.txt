Police Media Spokesperson SSP Ajith Rohana says that the involvement of a group of navy personnel has been uncovered in the disappearance of a group of youth between 2008 and 2009.
SSP Rohana noted that Navy Media Spokesperson Captain D.K.P.
Dassanayake was questioned by the Criminal investigations Department recently in connection with this revelation.
Police Media Spokesperson SSP Ajith Rohana said that there have been many incidents where children have been reported missing, especially in Mattakkuliya, Colombo between 2008-2009.
