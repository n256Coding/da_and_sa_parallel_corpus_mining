Sri Lankan fast bowler Suranga Lakmal has been fined during his side’s Pool A match in the ICC Cricket World Cup 2015 against England at the Wellington Regional Stadium on Sunday.
Suranga Lakmal was fined for firing two successive high full pitched balls at England’s Jos Buttler at the 50th over of the England’s innings ,after being given a first and final warning by on-field umpire Rod Tucker earlier during the same over.
Lakmal was stopped from bowling at Welington’s Westpace stadium and reported to the match referee David Boon for dangerous and unfair bowling.
Lakmal was fined 30 per cent (30%) of his match fee after pleading guilty to breaching rules that relate to ”Conduct Contrary to the spirit of the game.” .
