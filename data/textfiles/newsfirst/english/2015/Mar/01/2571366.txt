President Maithripala Sirisena says that the country will not sign any agreement which is deemed a threat to domestic entrepreneurs.
The President made this statement at a special meeting organised by committee members of Sri Lankan Youth Entrepreneurs in Battaramulla.
The meeting provided an opportunity to table issues and challenges faced by Sri Lankan entrepreneurs.
