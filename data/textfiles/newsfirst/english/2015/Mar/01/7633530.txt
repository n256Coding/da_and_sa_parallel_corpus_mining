Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs, Jeffrey Feltman has vowed his fullest support for the completion of the new government programme.
He made this statement while meeting with President Maithripala Sirisena on Saturday.
President Maithripala Sirisena said that the new government is committed to their new standing in national and international affairs.
Meanwhile, Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs, Jeffrey Feltman also carried out discussions with Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ajith P. Perera on Saturday.
The Deputy Minister said that the United Nations stand in relation to the national mechanism in order to look into alleged human rights violations during the war was clarified during the discussion.
The Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs will visit Jaffna on Monday.
He will also meet with the Governor and Chief Minister of the Northern Province.
