The Ministry of Public Administration, Provincial Councils and Local Government has decided to implement a new wage structure for public sector employees.
Ministry Secretary J. Dadallage, stated that the new salary structure will be implemented in collaboration with the Ministry of Finance.
Discrepancies have arisen among government employees due to the occasional salary increments and circulars that have been issued.
The Secretary to the Ministry stated that they are planning on eliminating these discrepancies with the new salary structure.
