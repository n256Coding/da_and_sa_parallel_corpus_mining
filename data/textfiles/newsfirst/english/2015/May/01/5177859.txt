The twenty second death anniversary of late President Ranasinghe Premadasa who is considered the first “commoner” to be at the helm of affairs, breaking a tradition of the top leadership being in the hands of the affluent – was marked today, Friday, May 1.
The main commemoration ceremony of the late president was held at the Premadasa statue in Hultsdorp, Colombo on Friday morning.
The twenty second commemoration ceremony which was organised in the vicinity of his statue in Colombo on Friday was held under the auspices of President Maithripala Sirisena.
