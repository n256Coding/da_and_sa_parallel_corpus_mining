A protest citing that demands of hotel employees be met, is currently being held opposite the Tourism Ministry in Colombo 3.
The protest has been organised by the Hotel Employees Centre of the Inter Company Employees Union.
The protesters lament that a solution to their issues related to work have not been met for years, after having directed the issue to the relevant authorities.
They further stated that four leading hotels in Colombo had to be shutdown.
They lamented that as their is no mention of their rights, their jobs have become unstable.
