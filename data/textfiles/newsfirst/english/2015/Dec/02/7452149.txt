According to Railway Department sources, the technical issue that had arisen with the railway signals located between the Maradana and Colombo Fort railway stations has been rectified.
It is also learnt that train services have now recommenced.
The Railway Control Room said that the issue had cropped up on Tuesday evening.
However, it is learned that the problem was looked into and solved last night.
As a result of the technical malfunction of the railway signals, there was a delay in train services along that rail route on Tuesday.
