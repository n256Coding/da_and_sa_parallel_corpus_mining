A police sergeant stationed at the Boralesgamuwa police station as the OIC of traffic has been arrested for allegedly accepting a bribe.
Investigation Officer of the Bribery Commission Priyantha Chandrasiri said that the alleged bribe was taken in order to not take legal action against a lorry that was transporting sand with an expired licence.
The suspected police officer was arrested at the instance where he allegedly accepted Rs.1,500 of a Rs.
5000 bribe allegedly requested by him.
