A debate took place in Parliament on Friday December 4, over the failure to provide parliamentarians with reports pertaining to state institutions for the previous years.
The Chief Opposition Whip Anura Kumara Dissanayake questioned on the requested reports on ministries, state departments and institutions for the last year, stating that they were not provided.
