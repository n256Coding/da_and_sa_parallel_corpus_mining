Delayed SriLankan flight from London to arrive today" The SriLankan Airline flight which had to be landed in Frankfurt Airport in Germany after a passenger fell ill, will arrive in Bandaranaike International Airport tonight.
Our airport correspondent said that the flight is scheduled to arrive at 8.30 pm.
Originally, it was scheduled to arrive at 12.57 pm.
The UL 504 flight with 268 passengers and 11 staff was forced to land at the German airport after a passenger suddenly fell ill. Two doctors who were among the passengers had checked the sick passenger and advised the flight staff to land the aircraft to treat him.
