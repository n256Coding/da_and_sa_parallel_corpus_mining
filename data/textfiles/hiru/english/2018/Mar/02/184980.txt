An 18-year-old schoolgirl sustained critical injuries when an unidentified object exploded inside a house in Maldenipura, Kekirawa.
She was first admitted to Kekirawa Hospital and then transferred to Anuradhapura Hospital for further treatment.
The explosion has caused injuries to girl's head and legs.
A few days ago, her father had brought an unidentified object to their home.
This accident has caused by its explosion.
Police have found several lead particles from the location where the explosion had occurred.
