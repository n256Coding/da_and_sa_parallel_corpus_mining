The Sergeant Major who was arrested for carrying the hand grenade that exploded inside a bus in Kahagolla on 21st of February was remanded until 16th of March.
He was arrested by Diyathalawa Police while he was receiving treatment at Diyathalawa Hospital and was produced before Bandarawela Magistrate.
19 persons were injured in the explosion and 12 of them were security forces personnel.
