The new State Ministers and Deputy Ministers will be sworn in at the Presidential Secretariat this morning (May 02).
The swearing-in ceremony will commence at 10.30 am.
All Ministers and MPs of the government have been requested to be present for this.
Eighteen new Cabinet Ministers were sworn in before President Maithripala Sirisena at a ceremony held at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday.
The President Media Division said that the other Cabinet Ministers will continue to serve in their existing portfolios.
