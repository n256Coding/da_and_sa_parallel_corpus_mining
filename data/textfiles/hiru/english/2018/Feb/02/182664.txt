The employees of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board they on a four-hour strike today (Feb. 02) demanding to resolve their salary issues.
The convener of the Joint Water Supply Trade Union Alliance, engineer Upali Ratnayake said they decided to take this initiative as the authorities are not responding to their demands.
Meanwhile issuing a communiqué, the National Water Supply and Drainage Board said that the management had held a discussion with trade unions to make a fair salary increase.
The communiqué said that it was decided at the meeting to hold discussions with Minister Rauff Hakeem to find a solution.
