Police say that two women were arrested after raiding a brothel house in Mount Lavinia.
It was operated in the guise of a massage clinic.
Police had carried out the raid after obtaining a search warrant from the Mount Lavinia Court.
The two women are aged 25 and 38 years and are residents of Ratnapura and Mount Lavinia.
The suspects will be produced before Mount Lavinia Court today (Feb. 02).
