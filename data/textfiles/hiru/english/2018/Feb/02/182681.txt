One person was killed and five others injured in a road accident which took place on the Weligama-Akuressa main road in the Bodagala area.
The accident occurred when a sand tipper collided head-on with a three-wheeler last afternoon (Feb. 01).
Six persons who were travelling in the three-wheeler were admitted to Walana District Hospital.
The driver succumbed to his injuries at the hospital, the police said.
