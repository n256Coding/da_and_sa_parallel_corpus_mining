With the dawn of the year 2018, triplets have been born at Dambulla Base Hospital.
The mother, who also has a 5-year-old boy, is from the Aravula area in Dambulla.
The triplets, all are girls, are in a healthy condition, the doctors said.
The mother said that her husband is working in a lorry in Colombo and they are faced with financial difficulties to look after their children now.
