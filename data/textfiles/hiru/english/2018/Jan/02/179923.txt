14.5 million which was being taken to be deposited at a bank was robbed in Nuwara Eliya this morning (Jan. 02).
Our correspondent said the money was being transported in a small lorry from a cigarette agency to be deposited at a private bank.
Then 2 unknown persons who came in a three-wheeler had blocked the road and had thrown chili powder at the lorry driver and the agency employee, before fleeing with a bag containing cash.
The incident took place at around 8 am today (Jan. 02).
Now Nuwara Eliya police have launched a special operation to apprehend the robbers.
