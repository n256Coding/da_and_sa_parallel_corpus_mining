The candidates contesting the upcoming Local Government polls under the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna have been called to Colombo today (Jan. 02).
Joint Opposition MP Keheliya Rambukwella said that everyone has been called to the Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium, Colombo to discuss the election campaign.
Similarly, UNP parliamentarians, as well as all its electoral organizers have been called to Colombo tomorrow.
That is to discuss the upcoming local government election.
This meeting is scheduled to be held at Temple Trees with Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at the chair.
