State Minister of Child Affairs Vijayakala Maheswaran says her intention is to see the reemergence of the LTTE.
She said this during an event held at Jaffna Weerasinham Hall today (Jul 02).
Ministers Vajira Abeywardena, Tilak Marapana and Chief Minister of North C. V. Vigneshwaran were present at the occasion.
Meanwhile, police have launched an investigation on an alleged incident where a banner depicting an Elam map was exhibited.
Police are looking out for the person who had been carrying out that banner.
