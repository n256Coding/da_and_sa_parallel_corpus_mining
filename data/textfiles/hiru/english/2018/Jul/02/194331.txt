The motor car used by the underworld gangsters to flee after the robbery at a jewellery shop in Matara was found in Thalagala, Moragahahena early this morning (Jul 02).
The Police Media Spokesperson SP Ruwan Gunasekera said that one suspect was also arrested.
The preliminary investigations revealed that the vehicle was registered under the name of the suspect's wife.
He was a three-wheel driver.
Police also said that the suspect was very close to Kosgoda Tharaka, who was injured during the exchange of fire with police.
It was revealed that the robbery was planned at the suspect's house in Thalagala.
