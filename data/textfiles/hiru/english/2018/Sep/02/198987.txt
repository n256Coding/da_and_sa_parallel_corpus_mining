Police have arrested a 27-year-old person in relation to the murder of 23-year-old female police constable at Haliela, Badulla.
Police said that the suspect, who was the lover of the victim had slit the neck before setting fire on her.
It was found that the murdered woman was attending an almsgiving ceremony at a nearby house, then she had returned home, with a lunch packet to the suspect.
The neighbors have located the body after they came to her home seeing smoke.
The suspect was working as a field officer at a tea estate.
