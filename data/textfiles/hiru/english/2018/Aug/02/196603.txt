Dambulla Police arrested 10 persons who behaved in an unruly manner during the ODI match between Sri Lanka and South Africa at the Dambulla International Cricket Stadium last night.
Police said a security guard who was on duty at the vehicle park and UNP Central Provincial Councillor Tissa Bandara Herath who received injuries were admitted to Dambulla Hospital.
The injured security guard, Chaminda Pushpakumara told us that one of the spectators attacked him with a glass bottle.
Later, the riot police arrested the attacker.
Central Provincial Councilor, Tissa Bandara Herath said that he was also assaulted when he had intervened to settle the dispute.
