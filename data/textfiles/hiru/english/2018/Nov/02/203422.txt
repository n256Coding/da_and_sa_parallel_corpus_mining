Speaker Karu Jayasuriya says that President Maithripala Sirisena has informed him to reconvene Parliament on November 07 th .
He was speaking in a meeting with a group of political party representatives at parliament complex this morning.
Earlier, the UNP, Ilangai Tamil Arasu Kachchi, the JVP and the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress wrote to the speaker requesting to convene parliament.
UNF MPs Palani Digambaram, Mano Ganeshan, Rauff Hakeem and Rishard Bathiudeen were present at the meeting.
Opposition Leader, R Sampandan and JVP MPs Vijitha Herath and Nalinda Jayatissa also participated.
Later, UNP General Secretary, Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said that 118 MPs were present at the meeting held today.
