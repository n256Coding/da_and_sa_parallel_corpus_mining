Former Minister, Patali Champika Ranawaka engaged in some routine duties at his office on the Suhurupaya in Battaramulla today despite the previous Cabinet being dissolved.
Placing a post on his Facebook account, former minister Ranawaka states that he met his officials and had discussions with them.
In addition, Primary Industries Minister, Daya Gamage has also visited his office and held discussions with his staff today.
Meanwhile, the GMOA threatens to launch a strike if the former Health Minister Rajitha Senarathna is appointed to the same portfolio in the new Cabinet.
