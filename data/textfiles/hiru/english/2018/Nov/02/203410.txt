Former Defence Secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa has met former Prime Minister Ranil Wickkramasingha at Temple Trees.
Reports say that this meeting had taken place on the request of Ranil Wickremesinghe.
At this meeting the former defence secretary had said that he was ready to provide full security to Ranil Wickremesinghe at the time of leaving Temple Trees.
Former Defence Secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa has said that such security could be ensured even after he leaves Temple Trees.
