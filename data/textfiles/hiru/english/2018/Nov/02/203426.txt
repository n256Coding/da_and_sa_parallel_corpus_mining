Transport and Aviation Minister, Nimal Siripala de Silva said that another group of ministers are scheduled to take oaths today as well.
Addressing a media conference held at the SLFP Headquarters, Minister Silva said that the new Cabinet will meet on Monday.
Meanwhile, Government media spokesman, Mahinda Samarasinghe said that the Prime Minister will present an interim Vote of Account substituting to the budget during the forthcoming session of parliament.
