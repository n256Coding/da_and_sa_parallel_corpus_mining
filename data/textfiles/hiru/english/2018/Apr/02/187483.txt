Former DIG Vass Gunawardena and 5 others who are accused of having in possession firearms illegally, were released on bail today (Apr.
02).
The case relating to this was taken up at the Colombo High Court.
They were granted bail on a cash bail of Rs.
10,000 and 2 personal bails of Rs.
200,000 each.
Earlier, they had submitted a revised bail application.
DIG Vass Gunawardena was earlier found guilty of murdering businessman Mohammed Shyam.
