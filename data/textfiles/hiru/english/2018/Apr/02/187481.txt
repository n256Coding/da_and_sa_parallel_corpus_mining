Police say that an unidentified group has forcibly entered a house and assaulted two women in the East of Vadamaarchchi.
The 2 women were a daughter and mother and the 56-year-old daughter had died due to the attack that took place early this morning (Apr.
02).
The 78-year-old mother is receiving treatment at the ICU of the Jaffna Hospital.
Meanwhile, the area residents staged a protest urging the authorities to arrest the attackers.
