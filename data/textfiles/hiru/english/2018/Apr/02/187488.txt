A FlyDubai flight which was en-route to Dubai from Bandaranaike International Airport via Mattala was delayed by 5 hours due to a bird strike.
The flight had left the BIA with 113 passengers on board and it was to get 55 more passengers from the Mattala International Airport.
While it was on the way to Mattala, the flight has encountered with a bird strike.
Our correspondent said that the passengers were inconvenienced due to the lack of facilities at the Mattala airport.
