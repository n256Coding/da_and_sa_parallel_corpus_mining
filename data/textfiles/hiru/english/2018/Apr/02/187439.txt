Frontline Socialist Party says the existing issues faced by the masses have been created by the present political culture.
The Organizing Secretary of the FSP, Kumar Gunaratnam said this addressing a public forum in Badulla yesterday (Apr, 01).
He pointed out the removal of PM through the 'no-confidence motion', bond scam and solving the issues in the electoral system would not support in clearing the economic issues.
He said the only solution is to change the existing political culture in the country.
