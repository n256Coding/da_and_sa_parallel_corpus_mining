An 18-year-old student of Trinity College, Kandy who had collapsed during a school rugby training session yesterday, had died at the Kandy Hospital today (02).
He was a grade 12 student.
Our Kandy correspondent said that the student had taken part in a road run as a part of training and then had collapsed on the road.
He had died at the Kandy Hospital while receiving treatment.
Incidentally, the victim was a son of a Trinity College’s non-academic staffer.
