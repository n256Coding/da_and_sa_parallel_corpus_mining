A 21-year-old died in a head-on collision between a motorcycle and a cab in the Pudukuduirippu area in Mullaithivu.
The cab was belonging to Pudukuduirippu Pradeshiya Sabha.
The youth who sustained serious injuries was admitted to Mullaitivu Hospital and Police said he died at the hospital.
The victim was a resident of Pudukuduirippu, a member of Civil Defence Force.
