A protest was held in the Padaviya town this morning (02) urging the authorities to solve the wild elephant attacks.
Villagers of Konkatiyawa, Kumbukwewa, Mylankulama and Aluth Halmillewa took part in the protest.
They said a single wild elephant is roaming in their villages, obstructing their day-to-day activities.
Later, the protestors marched towards the Padaviya Divisional Secretariat and handed over a letter to the Divisional Secretary.
