The President of the "Sinhala Ravaya" organization, Venerable Akmeemanan Dayarathana Thera has arrived at the Colombo Crimes Division.
The Thera was requested to visit the CCD to record a statement on the recent incident in Mount Lavinia.
Meanwhile, Venerable Arambepola Rathanasara Thera has also been requested to visit the CCD today (02).
