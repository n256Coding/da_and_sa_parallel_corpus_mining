The President has instructed the cabinet to take immediate action to determine whats happened to the narcotics that were apprehended from various raids.
The president requested that a detailed report be submitted to him without delay .
The president also appointed a cabinet sub-committee comprising of ministers Sagala Ratnayake, Wijedasa Rajapaksa and Susil Premajayanta to oversee the investigation.
The cabinet met with President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday.
