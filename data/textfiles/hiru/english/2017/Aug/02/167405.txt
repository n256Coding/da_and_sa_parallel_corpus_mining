Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption has arrested a Police Constable who works attached to the Pelmadulla Police.
He has been arrested due to attempting to take bribes.
It has been reported that this police Constable has asked for a bribe amounting to Rs.6000 to drop a legal case against a Three Wheeler accident.
The suspect has kept the National ID and the Driving License of the driver until the bribr is received.
Later, the suspect has been arrested following a complaint made by the Three-Wheeler driver regarding the case.
