It was revealed during the media conference to inform Cabinet decisions today that the President, the Prime Minister or the Foreign Minister were unaware of the communiqué issued by the Foreign Ministry with regard to the recent firing of a ballistic missile by North Korea.
North Korea carried out a ballistic missile test on July 28th.
Later, the Foreign Ministry issued a communiqué condemning the missile test citing that such hostile action would threaten international peace and security.
Answering journalists, Joint Cabinet spokesman minister Dayasiri Jayasekera said that an inquiry has already been initiated over the issuing of the communiqué.
