The ministry of  Education made the decision to close down schools in eight districts due to those areas being impacted by floods and landslides.
at a media briefing in Colombo yesterday minister of Education  Akila Viraaj Kaariyawasam said the decision regarding when these schools should be reopened would be made known today.
the Minister said that these schools would not be reopened until a certificate of quality assurance  for the buildings was produced.
