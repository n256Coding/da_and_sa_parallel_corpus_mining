Met Department has announced that showers will occur at times in the Western, Sabaragamuwa, Southern, Central and North-western provinces.
Showers or thundershowers will occur at several places in the Eastern, Uva, North-central provinces and in the Vauniya districts, particularly in the afternoon.
Fairly strong winds about 50 kmph can be expected during showers.
General public is kindly requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
