President Maithripala Sirisena stated that it is the responsibility of the government to ensure that the Youth don’t take weapons.
This was joining the closing ceremony of Yowunpura – the National Youth Ceremony in Trincomalee yesterday.
Nearly 6,000 youth including foreign participants took part in the ceremony.
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe also joined the event.
President Maithripala Sirisena addressing the event stated that it is the responsibility of the rulers of the country to handover the task of building the country to the Youth.
President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday engaged in an inspection tour of the Kinniya Base Hospital.
A protest was being held demanding the hospital be renovated and the President responded positively.
