A police constable and a sergeant attached to Dodamgoda police were suspended in connection to brutally assaulting a motorcyclist at Dodamgoda Junction in Kalutara.
Recently, footage of the two police officers assaulting a motorcyclist who did not have a driving license and disobeyed orders to stop the motorcycle was released by media.
The police officers were suspended after investigating into the incident.
The youth who was assaulted is currently receiving treatment at Nagoda Hospital in Kalutara.
