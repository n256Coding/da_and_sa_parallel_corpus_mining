Met Department announced that showers or thundershowers will occur at most parts of the island after 2pm.
Showers can be expected Western, and Sabaragamuwa provinces and in Galle, Matara and Jaffna districts too.
Heavy falls about 100mm are expected at some places, particularly in Central, Uva and North-central provinces and in Vavuniya, Mullaitivu and Trincomaleee.
In response to a query posed by the Hiru news team, Director of the Meteorological Forecasting Division, Anusha Warnasuriya stated that there may be temporary localized strong winds during thundershowers, and the general public is requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
