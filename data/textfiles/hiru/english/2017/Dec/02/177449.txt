Six persons were injured, 2 critically when a van toppled into a precipice at Kalugahapura in Badulla this morning (02).
Police said the van with 10 occupants had veered off the road due to a faulty break-system.
The injured were admitted to Meegahakiula Hospital and thereafter, 2 of them were transferred to Badulla Hospital.
The occupants of the van were from Panagoda and they were returning after attending a party.
