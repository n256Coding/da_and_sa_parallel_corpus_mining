The Ministry of Home Affairs says that part of the compensation to be provided to those affected by natural disasters will be paid today (02).
Minister of Home Affairs Vajira Abeywardena said, therefore, all District Secretariats and Divisional Secretariats will be kept open today and tomorrow to facilitate this.
Accordingly, the affected people whose houses have been damaged will be able to obtain an advance payment out of the total compensation due to them.
Meanwhile, the Minister said that the people can inform the Ministry of any emergency situation through the number 1902.
