The Sri Lanka Police Narcotics Bureau at the Bandaranaike International Airport arrested a Pakistani national yesterday, who was attempting to smuggle into the country, over 2 kilograms of heroin.
Police said the 46-year-old suspect, who arrived at BIA from Doha yesterday, had in his possession 2.136 kilograms of heroin, The stash has a street value of approximately about Rs.
2 million.
