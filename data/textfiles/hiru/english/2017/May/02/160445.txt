Dead body of the youth who went missing when bathing in Dimbula-Kothmale Oya has been found.
According to our correspondent, dead body has been found with the help of Dimbula Police and Nuwara Eliya Police Life Savers' Force.
The deceased has been identified as a 20 year-old resident in an estate in Dimbula.
The body has been found in a pit in the reservoir close to where he has bathed.
