Sri Lanka Port Management and Advisory Services Pvt.
ltd accuses the Joint Opposition of littering the Galle Face Green yesterday at which the May Day Rally of the party was held.
Further, the company says the litter has not been cleaned yet.
Chief Executive Officer of Sri Lanka Port Management and Advisory Services Pvt.
Ltd stated that the written condition extended over the Rally organizers have been hereby broken by the party.
Moreover, in response to Hiru news team, the convener of Joint Opposition, MP Prasanna Ranathunge voiced that they removed the litter from morning.
