One person has died by colliding with a bus in Pidurangala area in Sigiriya.
The accident has taken place at about 2.15am in the early morning.
This person has been on his way back home from the UNP May Rally held in Cambel Grounds and he has slipped from the footboard and the bus has run over over him.
He has been admitted to the Dambulla hospital and later has died.
The deceased is a 44 year-old resident in New Village in Sigiriya.
Police has arrested the driver of the bus.
