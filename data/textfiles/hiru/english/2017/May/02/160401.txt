4 women walking along the shores off the Chilaw coastline, were swept away by a strong current last evening.
It has been reported that they were taking photographs with their backs to the sea shore.
The four were abruptly swept away, 3 of them were rescued.
However a 21 year old is still reported missing.
The women are residents of Chilaw- Ilippadeniya.
Police and Navy officers from Kalpitiya have commenced search operations to locate the missing woman.
