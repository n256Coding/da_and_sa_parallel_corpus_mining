Archbishop of Colombo, Malcom Cardinal Ranjith has requested all Catholic private schools across the island to remain closed next week, until further notice.
Meanwhile, Minister of Education Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, stated that a special security plan will be in operation from the 6th of this month, when schools across the country reopen.
Meanwhile, the Sri Lanka Army has initiated a campaign to distribute flyers among students across the country, with the intention of creating awareness about suspicious parcels.
Archbishop of Colombo, Malcom Cardinal has requested not to conduct mass at Catholic Churches this coming Sunday as well.
