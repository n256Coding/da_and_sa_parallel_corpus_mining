The Court ruling on whether to deport drug kingpin Makandure Madush has been further postponed until May 9 by the Dubai Court.
Madush filed a motion requesting the court not to deport him from Dubai saying that his life would be at risk in Sri Lanka.
Meanwhile actor Ryan Van Rooyan who was remanded after being deported by Dubai police was further remanded until May 9th.
He was produced before the Matara Magistrate today.
Meanwhile, Amila Prasanna Hettihewa alias Sunshine Suda who was released after being deported by Dubai police was arrested yesterday with 4 grams of heroin in his possession.
Later, he was produced before the Court and was remanded until May 16.
