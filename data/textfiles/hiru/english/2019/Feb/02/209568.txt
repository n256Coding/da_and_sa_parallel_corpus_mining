Leader of the Opposition, Mahinda Rajapaksa stated that the government is attempting to form another national government with the intention of increasing the number of minister posts, thereby to secure 113 seats in parliament.
The opposition leader made this statement speaking to media after paying courtesy calls on the chief prelates of the Malwathu and Asgiri chapters this morning.
Afterwards, the Leader of the Opposition paid a courtesy call on the Chief Prelate of the Ramagna Nikaya, Venerable Napane Pemasiri Thera at the Koorikaduwa Vidya Sagara Pirivena in Menikhinna.
