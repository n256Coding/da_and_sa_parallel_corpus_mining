A Sri Lankan father has shot and attempted to kill his 26-year-old daughter in the Tiollo area of Padua Italy.
According to Italian media reports, the 46-year-old father has tried to commit suicide after he shot and attempted to kill his 26-year-old daughter.
It is reported that he tried to hang himself after his attempt to shoot himself failed.
However, the Italian police intervened to save the daughter and her father.
The daughter’s condition is stable but the father’s condition is critical according to the papers in Italy that has quoted from the hospital sources.
