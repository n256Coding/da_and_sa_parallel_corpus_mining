The Disaster Management centre has advised fishermen who are fishing in the Bay of Bengal to evacuate from the area and to go to a safe area immediately.
The Department of Meteorology stated that the wind speed may increase up to 65 kmph from time to time in the sea areas off the coast extending from Puttalam to Mullaitivu via Kankesanthurai.
The Department requests the fishing and naval community to be vigilant as the sea areas will be rough at times.
Meanwhile, several spells of showers are expected in the Western, Sabaragamuwa and Southern provinces.
According to the weather report thundershowers will develop after 2 pm at several places in the Eastern, Uva and Central Provinces and Polonnaruwa District.
