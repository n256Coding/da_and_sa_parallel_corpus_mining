The transfer of Kurunegala DIG Kithsiri Jayalath has been cancelled.
The National Police Commission Secretary, Nishantha Weerasinghe told our news team that the Police Commission has taken this decision due to the prevailing situation in the country.
On the recommendation of the Acting IGP, the national police commission approved the transfer of the Kurunegala DIG to Trincomalee.
However, the reset of the transfers of 17 DIGs except for the Kurunegala DIG have been approved.
