The police say that the person who shot yesterday at the Maligakanda area in Maradana has been charged with several criminal offenses.
It is also reported that a financial dispute with the injured person led to the shooting.
It is said that the suspect had entered a house in Maligakanda Road last night with a firearm.
It is reported that a heated argument had taken place between the owner of the house and the suspect over a financial matter.
After the argument, the suspect tried to flee, shooting to the ground.
As a result, another person in the house was injured.
He was admitted to the Colombo National Hospital for treatment.
The victim is a 77 year old resident of Wellampitiya.
