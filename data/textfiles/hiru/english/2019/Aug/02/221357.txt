A man has been killed by a wild elephant attack in Walpaluwa area in Anamaduwa.
Police said that the man had been attacked this morning by the wild elephants while he was working behind the house.
He was critically injured and succumbed to his injuries on admission to the Anamaduwa base hospital.
The victim was a 54-year-old resident of the Leekolawewa area in Mahakumbukkadawala.
