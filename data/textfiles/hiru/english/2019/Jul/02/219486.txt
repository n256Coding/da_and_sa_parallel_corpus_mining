Railway trade unions have decided to obtain legal counsel regarding continuing strike action.
Accordingly trade union representatives and attorneys are to meet and discuss at 10 am regarding the matter.
Train services being gazette as an essential service and the emergency law being in effect has resulted in the situation.
Several employees serving different grades in railway service including railway engine drivers commenced a strike recently standing against the cabinet approved proposals on eliminating salary anomalies, not been brought into effect.
Later strike action was initiated every Thursday from midnight for a period of 24 hours.
Following this the government issued a gazette notification, making train engine driving, road maintenance, signaling and operations, ticket issuing essential services.
