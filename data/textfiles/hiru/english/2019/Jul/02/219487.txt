The buttala police have shot at an individual who ran over a police officer and  fled the scene while riding a motorcycle intoxicated in the Buttala city.
Police noted that owing to the shooting, the suspect had sustained injuries in his leg, and he has been admitted to the Sirigala hospital for treatment.
The incident occurred when the suspect and another individual were traveling on a motorcycle in the Buttala city last evening.
However police noted that the police officer was not harmed due to the incident.
