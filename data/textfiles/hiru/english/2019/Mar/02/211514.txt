Police arrested K D Lal Kantha, member of the JVP Political Bureau and former MP over a vehicular accident in Anuradhapura yesterday.
Police said Lal Kantha hit a motorcycle head-on while driving his cab near the Anuradhapura Prison on the Anuradhapura-Kurunegala road.
K D Lal Kantha will be produced before the Anuradapura Magistates today.
Preliminary investigations revealed that K D Lal Kantha was under the influence at the time of the accident.
A woman and a girl riding on the motorcycle have been injured in the accident and admitted to the Anuradhapura hospital.
