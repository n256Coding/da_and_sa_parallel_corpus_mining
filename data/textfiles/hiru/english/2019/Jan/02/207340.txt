The purviews coming under each ministry was discussed during the maiden Cabinet meeting held this morning.
However, the President has told that he would issue a new gazette with the amendments to such purviews in the near future.
A senior cabinet minister told our news team that the Prime Minister has already forwarded the amendments to the president.
Meanwhile, Minister Daya Gamage said that the government will be able to present the budget for 2019 at the end of February.
He was answering journalists at the end of the Cabinet meeting today.
