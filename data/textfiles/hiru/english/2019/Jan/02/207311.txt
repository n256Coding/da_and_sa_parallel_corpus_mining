The first Cabinet meeting for 2019, took place this morning at the Presidential Secretariat.
The meeting was presided by president maitripala Sirisena, Education Minister, Akila Viraj Kariyawasam stated that Cabinet approval had been granted to have the Enterprise Sri Lanka development program relaunched this year.
A Cabinet paper presented by the Finance Minister relating to approval for a Budget has been postponed for the next Cabinet meeting.
Meanwhile, MP Bandula Gunawardana stated that the people cannot expect economic relief from the budget that has been proposed by the government.
