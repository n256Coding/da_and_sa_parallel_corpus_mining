The central bank governor, Dr Indrajith Coomaraswamy stated that the ongoing forensic auditing on the central bank bond scam will be completed before the end of this year.
Earlier, President Maithripala Sirisena had informed the Central Bank governor to complete the forensic auditing on the central bank bond scam without delay.
The Central Bank governor announced this during a media conference held at his office in Colombo this morning.
