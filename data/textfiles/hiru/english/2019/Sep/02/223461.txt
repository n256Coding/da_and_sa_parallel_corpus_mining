The head of the terrorist who attacked the Zion Church on Easter Sunday, buried in the Kalliyankadu Hindu cemetery in Batticaloa has been exhumed.
This was in the presence of the Batticaloa Magistrate A.C. Rizwan.
The order was given by the Magistrate's Court recently based on the submissions of the Criminal Investigation Department.
The Batticaloa Magistrate has ordered the District Secretary of Batticaloa to exhume the remains of the terrorist from the place where he was buried and to identify a specific location suitable for reburial and to deposit the remains in the new location.
