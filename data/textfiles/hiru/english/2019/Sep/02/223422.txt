The Parliamentary Select Committee appointed to look into the terrorist attacks on Easter Sunday is scheduled to meet in Parliament today at 3.30 pm for a discussion amongst the committee members.
This is to obtain the views and recommendations of the committee members in relation to the preparation of the final report.
Meanwhile, the Minister of Education, Akila Viraj Kariyawasam is due to appear before the Presidential Commission of inquiry appointed to investigate into acts of corruption & frauds occurred in state institutions from 2015-2018, at 9.30 am.
This is in relation to the complaint made by the Supplies Director of the Ministry of Education, I.M.K.B.
Illangasinghe regarding the reopening of a disciplinary inquiry file against him by the Minster of Education.
