Former President of the Sri Lanka Medical Council Professor Carlo Fonseka has passed away.
He was 86 years old when he passed away after a brief illness this afternoon in his home.
Meanwhile, Piyasara a renowned traditional Sri Lankan drummer and a traditional dance lecturer in the country has passed away today.
He was 75 years old and he passed way in his home in Hokandara.
He also served as a Visiting Lecturer at the Universities of Colombo and Sri Jayewardenepura and served as a Senior Lecturer at the University of Kelaniya
