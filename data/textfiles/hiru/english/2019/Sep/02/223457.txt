The 17 suspects involved in the murder of two businessmen in Ratnaudagama area in Rathgama Galle have been remanded until the 16th of this month.
This was when the case was taken up before the Galle Chief Magistrate Harshana Kekunwela today.
The suspects have been arrested in connection with the abduction, murder and burning of the two businessmen on 23 January.
Out of the 17 suspects, 15 are police officers of the Special Criminal Investigation Unit of the Southern Province and the remaining two suspects are officers of the Forest Conservation Department.
