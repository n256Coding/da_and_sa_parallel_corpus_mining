A Sri Lankan with ‘Wallapatta’ has been taken into custody by the Katunayake Airport customs officials.
The 22-year-old, who was travelling to Dubai, was taken into custody by the Airport Customs with 22 kilograms and 95 grams of Wallapatta.
In addition, 300 grams of Wallapatta oil was also found in his possession.
Customs confiscated the stock of Wallapatta and the suspect was fined Rs.
50,000.
