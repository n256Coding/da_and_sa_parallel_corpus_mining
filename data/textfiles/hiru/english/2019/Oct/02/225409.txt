The petition seeking an order from court to be issued to the Internal & Home Affairs Minister Vajira Abeywardena against recognizing the citizenship of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna presidential candidate Gotabhaya Rajapaksa, is to be taken up before a three-judge bench appeal court today.
The petition was taken up before Justices Yasantha Kodagoda and Arjun Obeysekera of the Court of Appeal on the 30 th .
Considering the importance of the petition, it was stated that the hearing would take place before a three-judge bench.
