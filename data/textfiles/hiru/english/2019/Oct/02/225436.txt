The Meteorology Department forecasts thundershowers in the sea areas off the coast extending from Mannar to Colombo and Galle via Matara during the next 24 hours.
The Department stated that temporary strong winds could be experienced in the region of 70 to 80 kilometers per hour during thundershowers.
Meanwhile, the Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, North Western and Uva provinces, as well as the districts of Galle, Matara and Anuradhapura could experience rainfall of more than 75 mm after 2 pm.
The Department has advised the public to be vigilant of lightning accidents that could occur during thundershowers.
