Muttiah Muralitharan, the former Sri Lankan legendary cricketer states that there is no truth to the reports being published about him on social media.
Muralitharan made this statement in response to a query from Hiru news regarding the social media reports that he would contest the next general election.
Muttiah Muralitharan stated that he has never made any statement regarding entering politics.
He also stated that he categorically rejects all reports stating that he will contest the next general election.
Muralitharan requested sports fans who love him to stop sharing such false reports on social media.
