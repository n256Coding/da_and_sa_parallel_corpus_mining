A person has been arrested with heroin, hashish and ice in the Talaimannar - rural area.
The Dangerous Drug Unit of the Mannar Police Division inspected a house in the area yesterday.
The police seized 20 grams of heroin, 250 milligrams of hashish and 400 milligrams of ice from the house.
A 58-year-old man who was in the house was arrested at the time and during interrogation it was revealed that he had been involved in the drug trafficking business for some time.
