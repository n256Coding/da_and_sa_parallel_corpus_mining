A fatal accident occurred on Ratnapura-Pelmadulla Main Road early this morning (02) when a private bus and a lorry had a head on collision, Police media sources reports.
A total of 11 people who got injured were admitted to Ratnapura Hospital.
One of the victims has died after being hospitalized.
The deceased was a 31-year-old resident of Namunukula area.
Police is conducting further investigations into the incident.
