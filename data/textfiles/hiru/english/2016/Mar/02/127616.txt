Deputy Inspector General (DIG) of Vavuniya Police U.K. Dissanayake, who was arrested on charges of illegal treasure hunting, was further remanded till March 09 by Vavuniya Magistrate M.L.A.
Manaaf today.
He was arrested after surrendered to police on September 19.
DIG Dissanayake was allegedly involved in illegal treasure hunting in Vavuniya.
