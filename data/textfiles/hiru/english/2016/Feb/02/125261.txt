All arrangements are made to celebrate Sri Lanka’s 68 th Independence Day, the Minister of Home Affairs Wajira Abeywardana said.
Under the theme ‘Ekama Deyak, Maha Balayak’ (One Nation, Great Power) the event will take place in Galle Face Green on Thursday (04).
A special bus service is also in place for the participants.
Meanwhile, traffic through roundabout at Galle Face Green to Parliament will be limited from 5am to 12pm.
