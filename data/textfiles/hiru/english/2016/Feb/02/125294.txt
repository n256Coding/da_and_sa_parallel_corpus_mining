The widow of Sumith Prasanna Jayawardena, who died following a clash with the police in Embilipitiya in January, filed a Fundamental Rights (FR) petition with the Supreme Court, demanding Rs.
10 million as compensation for her husband's death.
She has named ten respondents in her petition including the former ASP of Embilipitiya D.W.C.
Dharmaratne, the IGP and the Attorney General.
