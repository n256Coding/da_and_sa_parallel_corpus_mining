Showery weather and cloudy skies is expected today due to the low level atmospheric disturbance in the Bay of Bengal, the Department of Meteorology stated.
Heavy falls (more than 100 mm) can be expected at some places, particularly in the Southern, Central, Western and Sabaragamuwa provinces until 7th November, it added.
Highest rainfall with the past 24 hours was reported in Kurunegala with 133mm.
