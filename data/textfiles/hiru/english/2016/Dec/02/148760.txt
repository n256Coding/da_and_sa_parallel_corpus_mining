The President's Media Unit said Vice President-elect of the United States of America Mike Pence, had phoned President Maithripala Sirisena last night and invited him to visit the US in a bid to enhance bilateral relations between the two nations.
President Sirisena had extended his good wishes to US President-elect Donald Trump and Pence during the phone conversation.
Pence has commended the achievements of Sri Lanka and the progress which has been made.
He has also assured him that the US stands ready to provide any assistance to Sri Lanka.
