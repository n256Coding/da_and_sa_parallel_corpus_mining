A special commemoration  ceremony to pay tribute to former Cuban leader late Fidel Castro was held at the President’s House today.
Addressing the gathering, President Maithripala Sirisena said that every politician in the world has ample lessons to learn from late Castro’s life.
President Sirisena said that the late Cuban leader who valued freedom, democracy, justice and equality set an extraordinary example to the world.
A former president Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga, Cuban ambassador to Sri Lanka Juana Ramos, MPs D.E.W.
Gunesekera, Dinesh Gunawardana, Wasudeva Nanayakkara and Tissa Vitharana were also present at the event.
