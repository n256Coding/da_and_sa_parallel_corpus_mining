A student attached to the Sabaragamuwa University was killed last night after being run over by a bus in front of Gandara Police Station.
The ill-fated bus was transporting a group of Joint Opposition supporters back home after their May Day rally.
The 26-year-old victim of Gandara Talalla met was hit by the bus in front of the Gandara Police station.
Police Media spokesman office stated that the driver of the bus was arrested this morning while he was in hiding.
