An individual, who was in the kitchen, has died after being struck by lightning in Bulathkohupitiya police division last evening, Police Media sources stated.
The deceased is a 34-year-old resident of Undugoda area.
The body of the deceased is kept at the morgue of Undugoda Hospital and the post mortem examination will be conducted today, Police added.
Bulathkohupitiya Police is conducting further investigations into the incident.
