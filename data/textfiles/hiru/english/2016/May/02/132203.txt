Former president Mahinda Rajapaksa said he and some others will be taken to courts for taking part at the May Day rally organized by the Joint Opposition.
He made this statement whilst addressing the gathering at the Lalith Athulathmudali grounds in Kirulapone yesterday.
Forty Parliamentarians including Dalus Alahapperuma, Mahinda Yap Abewardena, Mahindananda Aluthgamage, Bandula Gunawardena and Pavithra Wanniarachchi participated at the rally.
Politicos representing other parties were also seen at the event.
