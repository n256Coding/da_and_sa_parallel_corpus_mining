Three people have drowned to death during the past 24 hours.
Two of them who are residents of Galle have demised while bathing in a natural pool called ‘Kalu Wala’ in Bambarawana, Elpitiya.
Meanwhile, a 17 year-old boy has also drowned to death in Thalalla beach.
The Police noted that the teen is a resident of Devinuwara.
