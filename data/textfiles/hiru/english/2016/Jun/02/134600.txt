Former ASP of Embilipitiya D.W.C.
Dharmarathna, who was remanded over the death of a youth in Embilipitiya, was released on bail today.
He was released on two surety bail of Rs.
500,000 each and Rs.
20,000 of cash bail.
On February 02, he was arrested by the CID over the death of Sumith Prasanna of Embilipitiya.
