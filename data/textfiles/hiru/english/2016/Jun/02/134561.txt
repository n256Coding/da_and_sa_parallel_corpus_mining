Northern Province Chief Minister C. V. Vignesvaran says that many expectations of the people from the North were not fulfilled by the current government.
The Chief Minister said so when he met visiting Norway Deputy Foreign Minister Toray Hatram at the Chief Minister’s office in Jaffna yesterday.
While the delegation including Norway Secretary of State on a two-day visit to Sri Lanka arrived in the island, the Norwegian Deputy Foreign Minister also toured the Northern Province yesterday.
During the tour the Norwegian Secretary of State first met Northern Province Governor Reginald Cooray.
Subsequently the Norwegian Secretary of State held a discussion with   Northern Chief Minister C. V. Vignesvaran.
