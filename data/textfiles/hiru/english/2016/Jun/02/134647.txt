President Maithripala Sirisena emphasizes that no one will be allowed to put up unauthorized constructions or land fillings.
He states this while participating at a Committee Meeting held at Gampaha District.
President Maithripala Sirisena presided over the Gampaha District special Development Coordinating Committee meeting which was held at the Faculty of Education of Siyane, today (02).
The President looked into the issues faced by the people of Gampaha District.
