The Lanka Private Bus Owners’ Association says that apart from the annual bus fare increment, the passengers should be charged an additional fare for traffic congestion from the 1st of July.
Chairman of the association Gamunu Wijeratne stated this while addressing a media briefing convened in Colombo yesterday (June 1st).
Elaborating on the matter, the Chairman of the Lanka Private Bus Owners’ Association noted that the minimum bus fare would be decided, after taking into account, traffic congestion.
