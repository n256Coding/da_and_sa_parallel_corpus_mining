A stock of Illegal alcohol and marijuana has been found in Sisilagama-Kegalle by the police.
The police headquarters stated that 4 suspects who were in possession of 1.5 kilograms of marijuana and 178 bottles of illicit liquor have been arrested.
The raid was conducted by OIC of the Rambukkana police and a team of officials under the instructions given to the Kegalle SSP by IGP Poojith Jayasundara.
