Acting Inspector General of Police (IGP) Pujith Jayasundara and Deputy Inspector General of Police (DIG) Gamini Mathurata arrived at the Police Commission for the inquiry into Police assault of Higher National Diploma in Accountancy (HNDA) protestors.
The commission summoned them to inquire about the recent assault by the police on the Higher National Diploma on Accountancy students who staged a protest near the UGC.
