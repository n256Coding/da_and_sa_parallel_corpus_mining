An International drug kingpin Mohammad Siddik has been further remanded until October 11 th .
Mohammad Siddik was remanded again today when he was produced before Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya.
Lawyers appeared for Mohammad Siddik requested bail but the CID objected the request.
The CID requested the Magistrate not to grant Siddik bail without counseling the Attorney General.
Mohammad Siddik who was arrested in Pakistan was brought to Sri Lanka by the CID on last 16 th March.
