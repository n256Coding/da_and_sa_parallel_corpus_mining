The strike commenced by Ports Authority employees has been temporarily called off following an assurance given by the Chairman.
Workers attached to the Jaya Container terminal and Samagi Terminal of the Ports Authority commenced a strike claiming that the relevant authorities were not paying them the incentive allowance, which is offered by the shipping firms.
Member of the Ports Authority Jaya Container Terminal’s Workers’ Association Upali Weerasekara stated that they called off the strike following an assurance made by the Chairman of the Ports Authority L Panagoda.
