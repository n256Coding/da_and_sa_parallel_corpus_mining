4 persons were injured in a clash which took place during a election campaign meeting in the Makulu-gas-wewa area in Galewala yesterday.
The meeting was organized by the JVP and Organizers stated that UPFA supports had attacked the rally.
JVP Matale District Leader Gamagedara Disanayake stated that the rally had to be called off.
