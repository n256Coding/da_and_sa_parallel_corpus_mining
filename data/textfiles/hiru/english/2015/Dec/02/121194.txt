National Peace and Reconciliation Office Chairperson, Former president Chandrika Bandaranayake Kumaranathunga (CBK) states that if the necessity arises she will seek the assistance of the international technology regarding the allegation of war crimes being committed in the country.
She stated the above at the media brief held at Colombo to inform the future programs of the National peace and reconciliation office.
The former president further stated that a new lesson in enhancing understanding among the nationalities will be introduced to the school syllabus.
