The new democratic front has notified the Elections Commissioner that they will contest the forthcoming Election under the Swan Symbol.
Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka and Current President Maithripala Sirisena contested for the Presidential elections in 2010 and 2015 under the Swan symbol representing the New Democratic front.
Meanwhile, Minister V Radakrishnan stated that the Upcountry Peoples front will contest the forthcoming General Election together with UNP.
