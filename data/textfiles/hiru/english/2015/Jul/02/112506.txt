The FCID questioned wife of Western Provincial Council Chief Minister Prasanna Ranatunga, Morin Ranatunga over 7 hours this morning.
Morin Ranatunga was questioned in connection with an allegation against Prasanna Ranatunga on a Rs.
5 million land scam.
Police Media Spokesman ASP Ruwan Gunasekera said that the police commenced questioning Morin Ranatunga at 10 this morning.
