Presidential candidate of the common opposition Maithreepala Sirisena says that no fee would be charged from university students under his government.
He was speaking at an election rally held in Katana yesterday.
UNP National Leader Ranil Wickremesinghe says that the prime aim of the Maithree government is to generate employment with handsome salaries to the people.
He was speaking at a public rally held in Borella last night.
