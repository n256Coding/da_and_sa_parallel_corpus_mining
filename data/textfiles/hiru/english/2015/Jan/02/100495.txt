A fuel bowser siphoning oil at a filling station in Nelumdeniya exploded and caught fire this afternoon.
Police Media Spokesman SSP Ajith Rohana said that no casualties were reported after the explosion.
However, fire fighters were summoned from Kandy to douse the blaze.
Meanwhile Police request the public to use alternative roads as Colombo Kandy road has been temporarily closed at Nelum-deniya.
