Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe says that it is time to prioritize the youth instead of political parties.
While attending a ceremony organized by the National Youth Council to distribute sports equipment, the Prime Minister stated that they have already commenced supporting the youth on short term basis.
A long term program is currently implemented to strengthen the education, career and income aspects of the youth generation.
The Prime Minister reiterated that measures should be taken to continue this program under the future parliament.
