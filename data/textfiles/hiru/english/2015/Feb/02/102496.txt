The US Assistant secretary of State for South and Central Asia Nisha Desai Biswal arrived in Sri Lanka today on a three-day-official visit.
During her three-day official visit, Nisha Biswal is scheduled to hold talks with President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, the Ministry of External Affairs reported.
She is also expected to tour the Northern part of the country as well.
