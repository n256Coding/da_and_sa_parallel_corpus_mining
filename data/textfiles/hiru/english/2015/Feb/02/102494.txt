Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe says that the budget, the Government presented this time is a ‘Saradiyel style’ budget.
Participating at a people’s rally held at the Mawanella town yesterday, the Prime Minister said that during the past 10 years the poor were taxed and the proceeds were given to the rich.
The Prime Minister said that method of taxing the poor and enriching the rich had been changed this time.
