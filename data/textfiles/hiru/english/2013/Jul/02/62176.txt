President Mahinda Rajapkase returned to the island this morning after a successful 5 day official visit to Tanzania and Seychelles.
Following bilateral discussions between President Mahinda Rajapaksa and his Seychelles counterpart James Alix Michel, Sri Lanka and Seychelles signed three agreements on air links, defence cooperation and vocational training.
During the visit, the two leaders had an in-depth exchange of views on Seychelles-Sri Lanka relations and major international and regional issues of mutual interest.
