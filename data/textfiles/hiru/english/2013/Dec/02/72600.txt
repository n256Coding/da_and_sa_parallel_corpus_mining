The Investigation with regard to the alleged assault on popular vocalist Saman de Silva at a music show in the Prison Sports ground in Borella on the 30th of last month is continuing.
Borella police have received 4 witnesses in connection with the alleged assault on the singer by Southern Provincial Council Agricultural Minister Bindu Weerasinghe.
Police Media Spokesman SSP Ajith Rohana told our news team that a special police team has been deployed to arrest Bindu Weerasinghe.
Prison Commissioner General Chandrarathna Pallegama said that an investigation has already been initiated on the instruction of Minister Chandrasiri Gajadheera.
