President Mahinda Rajapaksa states that history should be comprehended in order to build national harmony instead of sowing seeds of hatred.
President Rajapaksa made this observation while addressing a function which was held at the Welimada-Boralanda Sri Sudarshanarama Vihara today, held to unravel a newly constructed Buddha statue.
Speaking further President Rajapaksa added that Buddhist had always refrained from acting as extremists or racists.
