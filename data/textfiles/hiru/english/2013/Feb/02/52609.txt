Minister of Judicial Services Rauff Hakeem stated that the appointment of the new Secretary to the Judicial Services Commission is well within the constitutional frame work.
Minister Hakeem made this observation while addressing a function in Kandy today.
Speaking further Minister Hakeem had this to say.
Former Secretary of the Judicial Services Commission Manjula Thillekerathna was transferred to Rathnapura with effect from yesterday as a district judge while Sisira Rathnayeke was appointed as JSC Secretary.
