The UNP today requested the government to conduct a complete and comprehensive investigation regarding the mass grave in Matale.
General Secretary of the UNP MP Thissa Attanayaka made this statement while speaking at a press conference held in Colombo.
The JVP also expressed their views regarding this at a press conference held yesterday.
General Secretary of the JVP Tilvin Silva stated that the government would not be interested in conducting investigations regarding this mass grave which belonged to the period of the UNP government tenure.
