United Peoples Freedom Alliance leadership has decided to intensify its election campaign in the coming weeks especially in the northern province for the forthcoming provincial council election.
Senior minister Tissa Vitharana and the UPFA northern campaign leader Environment minister Susil Premjayantha were in Jaffna in the week end to spear head its strategies to have series of meeting in support of all 19 candidates contesting the Northern Provincial Council election due on September 21st .
