India’s popular anti-corruption activist Annaa Hasaare is getting ready for a Sri Lankan tour.
‘Times of India’ citing Annaa Hasaare’s lawyer said that most probably Annaa Hasaare’s Sri Lankan tour would take place this month.
‘Times of India’ said that the tour was taking place in accordance with an invitation from several civil organizations in Sri Lanka.
The newspaper said that in addition Annaa Hasaare would undertake a tour of Canada as well.
Annaa Hasaare carried out a fast in 2011demanding that an anti-corruption bill be passed in the Indian parliament.
As a result of this Annaa Hasaare is considered an anti-corruption activist in India
