President Mahinda Rajapaksa says that peace in the country cannot be built by digging into the past.
The President said so while participating in the function for inaugurating the Eastern Oluwil harbour.
Expressing his views further on this occasion President Mahinda Rajapaksa said that the Opposition was engaged in a false propaganda in the country today to the effect that the State banks were being sold.
The President pointed out that the era of sale of State property had ended and that the era of re-acquiring State property had now dawned.
