ollowing sponsorship extended by Rev Father Ranjith Abeywardene and Mrs Sujeewa Rathnayake of the 'Colombo Samanthi' charity organization, 19 Sri Lanka Light Infantry troops of the 65 Division under the supervision of the 651 Brigade Commander staged a project to distribute school accessories to students at Kaneshapuram Primary School on Tuesday (11).
Major General D.M.H.D Bandara, General Officer Commanding, 65 Division gave necessary guidelines in organizing this charity project for the benefit of poor students.
Troops of the Battalion took the initiative in this regard and gave away those accessories to students.
