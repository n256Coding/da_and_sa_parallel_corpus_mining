rigadier P.G.G.S Rathnayake assumed office as the 15th Brigade Commander of the Commando Brigade HQ in Ganemulla on Monday (6).
A Guard Turnout greeted the newly-appointed Brigade commander on arrival at the entrance.
Afterwards, Brigadier Rathnayake signed an official document to symbolize the acceptance of the appointment.
Several senior officers of the Commando Regiment, Staff officers, officers and other ranks gathered at the venue.
