rigadier S.M.S.P.B Samarakoon of Gajaba Regiment (GR) assumed office as the new Director of Veteran Affairs on Monday (30) at his office in the Army Headquarters at Sri Jayewardenepura.
The newly-appointed Director left his signature in an official document amid religious observances.
Staff officers of the Directorate, Officers and Other Ranks were present at the occasion.
