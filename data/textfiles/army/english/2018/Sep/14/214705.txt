 group of 17 Army personnel including 1 Officer responded to a request of the Yahalabedda Vidyalaya in Haputale and assisted the cleaning of the school playground surroundings and the road that leads to the playground on Sunday (9).
Major General Rukmal Dias, Commander, Security Forces - Central has directed the troops to attend to the project after the request to that effect was made by the Principal in the school.
Students also joined hands with Army troops.
