 awareness programme for troops at the Security Force Headquarters - Jaffna (SFHQ-J) on ‘Heart Attacks and Prevention’ was conducted by Dr Rajeewa Peiris, Senior Consultant Cardiothoracic Surgeon on Monday (6).
The programme was conducted under the guidance of Major General Darshana Hettiarachchi, Commander, Security Forces - Jaffna.
The visiting Senior Consultant educated the gathering on smoking, its effects and preventive measures.
General Officers Commanding, Brigade Commanders, Battalion Commanders, Officers and Other Ranks took part in the educative programme.
