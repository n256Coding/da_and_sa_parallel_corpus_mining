 lecture on ‘Archaeological History of Sri Lanka and Latest Archaeological Discoveries’ was organized by the Security Force Headquarters - Jaffna (SFHQ-J) in order to improve the knowledge on Sri Lanka’s history for Officers and Other Rankers in the Jaffna peninsula.
The lecture was conducted by Prof Raj Somadeva, Senior Lecturer (Archaeology) and Professor in Archaeology on Thursday (6) at the SFHQ-J.
Many Senior Officers, Officers and Other Rankers participated in the programme.
