he Forward Maintenance Area - East (FMA-E) marked its 25th anniversary on Saturday (14) in keeping with military traditions at its Headquarters in Minneriya.
On the anniversary day (14), Major General I. O. W Madola, Commander FMA-E was saluted in a Guard Turn Out and a Guard of Honour in the premises.
The FMA Commander planted a sapling in the premises and laid the cornerstone for construction of a new building for civil staff serving the FMA-E.
