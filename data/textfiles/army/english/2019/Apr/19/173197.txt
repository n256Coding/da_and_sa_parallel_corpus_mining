ilinochchi troops extended their assistance for conduct of the annual Kovil Pooja at Kanakambigai Amman Hindu Kovil on Friday (19).
Troops at the Security Force Headquarters offered refreshments to the devotees.
On the instructions of the Commander, Security Forces - Kilinochchi, Major General Vijitha Ravipriya, troops offered refreshments to the Hindu devotees who were participating in the Pooja ceremony.
More than 1000 devotees, attending the final segment of the ceremony received this Army hospitality.
