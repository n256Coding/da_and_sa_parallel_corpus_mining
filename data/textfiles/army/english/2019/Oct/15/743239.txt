9 Division Headquarters of the Security Force Headquarters - Mullaittivu (SFHQ-MLT), inaugurated its new Battalion Training School in Nanthikadal on Monday (14).
Major General Jayantha Seneviratne, Commander, Security Forces - Mullaittivu attended as the Chief Guest for the inaugural ceremony.
24 Sri Lanka Sinha Regiment troops began their training as the 1st Battalion of this Battalion Training School.
