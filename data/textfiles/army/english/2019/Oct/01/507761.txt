he 29th anniversary celebrations of the Sri Lanka Corps of Military Police (SLCMP) took place on Friday (27) at the SLCMP Regimental Headquarters in Narahenpita.
An entertaining feature with one of the popular beat bands, 'Update', comprised of vocalists representing all generations and dancing features, presented by 'Shine' performing artists added colour to the occasion.
Nearly, 70 Officers and 700 Other Ranks, including the Colonel Commandant, Centre Commandant, civil employees and several others watched the event.
