0 Army personnel including 6 Officers volunteered to offer their blood to patients in Kilinochchi Hospital on Thursday (01) when the Bank of Ceylon Kilinochchi branch held a blood donation to mark its anniversary.
The project was launched to mark the 80th BOC branch anniversary.
To sustain goodwill between civilians and members of the Security Forces, the Commander, Security Forces - Kilinochchi, Major General Vijitha Ravipriya assured his support to the project.
