 Sri Lanka Light Infantry troops in the 14 Division under the Security Force Headquarters-West assisted the distribution of dry ration packs among under privileged families in the general area of Angoda, Colombo on Sunday (11).
Dr Kelum Wijesinghe Foundation of Angoda, Himbutana sponsored the donation and it was inaugurated by Deshabandu Karu Jayasuriya, Speaker after a brief ceremony.
During the arrangement, more than 1300 needy families received those dry food packs.
