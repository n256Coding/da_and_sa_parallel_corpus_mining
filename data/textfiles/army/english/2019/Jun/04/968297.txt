 brief ceremony at the Security Force Headquarters - Jaffna (SFHQ-J) distributed 10 Desktop computers for use in all under command Brigades of the SFHQ-J on Monday (3) in order to further enhance efficiency.
Those computers were purchased utilizing SFHQ-J funds in order to assist under command Brigades to carry out their routine duties effectively.
Major General Dharshana Hettiarrachchi, Commander Security Force - Jaffna gave away those computers.
