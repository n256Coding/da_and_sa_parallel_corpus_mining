olonel M.G.A.N.B Mahathuwakkara of Sri Lanka Light Infantry Regiment assumed duties as the 8th Commander of the 592 Brigade on Thursday (12) at the Brigade Headquarters in Mathawalasinghekulam, Mullaithiuv.
To mark the memorable event, the new Brigade Commander planted a sapling in the Brigade Headquarters premises and signed a formal document to signify the acceptance of the new office.
Staff Officers of the Brigade Headquarters and Other Ranks graced the occasion.
