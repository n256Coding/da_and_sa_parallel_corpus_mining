rigadier W.B.S.M Abeysekara assumed office as the 12th Director of the Directorate of Rehabilitation on Wednesday (4) in his office at the Hekitta, Wattala.
He symbolized the acceptance of office by leaving his signature for an official document amid religious observances.
Senior officers including Commandants of Ranaviru Sevana & Mihindu Seth Medura (MSM), Deputy Director Rehabilitation, staff Officers and Other Ranks were present at the office.
