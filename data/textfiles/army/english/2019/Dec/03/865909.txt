n the request of the incumbent, Ven Kaagama Sirinanda Thero of Sirisamadhi Viharaya in Pahaminiyagama, 53 Division troops conducted a Shramadana to clean the temple premises and planted Sal saplings (Cannon ball) and a variety of fruit seedlings, medicinal herbs, etc on Saturday (30).
Major General P. J Gamage, General Officer Commanding, 53 Division, considering the request of this reputed Dhamma orator instructed a total of sixty Officers and Other Ranks to join the project and assist the leading monk.
Several members of the laity also joined hands with the troops.
